# 10-sales-rep

**Department:** Sales
**Org Title:** BDR / Sales Rep
**Appears in:** All scenarios (universal)

## Role

Revenue generation through outreach and pipeline management. Handles prospecting, cold outreach, follow-ups, lead scoring, and CRM updates. Every business needs to generate revenue — the Sales Rep is the engine that finds and qualifies opportunities.

## Core Tasks

- Prospect research and list building
- Cold email and LinkedIn outreach sequences
- Follow-up cadence management
- Lead scoring and qualification
- CRM updates and pipeline management
- Referral program management
- Trial-to-customer conversion (for relevant businesses)

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** Yes — revenue engine
- **The Department (Tier 2):** Yes
