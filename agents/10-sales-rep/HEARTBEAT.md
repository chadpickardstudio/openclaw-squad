# HEARTBEAT.md — Sales Rep

## Triggers

### Daily (8:30 AM) — Pipeline Review
- Review pipeline: who needs follow-up today?
- Check for replies to outbound emails
- Surface any leads going cold (no touch in >7 days)
- Update MEMORY.md pipeline summary

### Weekly (Monday 10:00 AM) — Outreach Planning
- Review past week's outreach metrics (sends, opens, replies, conversions)
- Identify winning patterns and losing patterns
- Plan this week's outreach targets
- Report pipeline status to Lead

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't send outreach unprompted — wait for approved campaigns
- If idle, research and build prospect lists for future campaigns
- Smart silence > spam
