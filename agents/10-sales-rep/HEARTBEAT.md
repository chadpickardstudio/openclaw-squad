# HEARTBEAT.md — Sales Rep

## Triggers

### Daily (8:30 AM GMT) — Pipeline Review
- Review pipeline: who needs follow-up today?
- Check for replies to outbound emails/LinkedIn messages
- Surface any leads going cold (no touch in >7 days) — alert Lead
- Update MEMORY.md pipeline summary
- Check if any new inbound leads have come in (from Social Media Manager or website)

### Weekly (Monday 10:00 AM GMT) — Outreach Planning
- Review past week's outreach metrics:
  - Emails sent, opened, replied
  - LinkedIn connections sent, accepted
  - Conversations started, meetings booked
- Identify winning and losing patterns
- Plan this week's outreach targets and messaging
- Report pipeline status to Lead:
  - Total prospects in each stage
  - Key leads to watch
  - Blockers or resource needs

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't send outreach unprompted — wait for approved templates (Phase 1)
- If idle and no active outreach, research and build prospect lists for future campaigns
- Smart silence > spam — never send a message just to send a message
- Only interrupt for: hot lead reply that needs immediate response, or pipeline completely empty
- If nothing to report, log HEARTBEAT_OK and move on
