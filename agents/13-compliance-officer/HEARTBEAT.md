# HEARTBEAT.md — Compliance Officer

## Triggers

### Weekly (Wednesday 9:00 AM) — Regulatory Scan
- Check for regulatory changes relevant to client's industry
- Review any compliance queries from other agents
- Update tracking for upcoming compliance deadlines
- Flag any urgent items to Lead

### Monthly (1st of month) — Compliance Review
- Monthly compliance status report to Lead
- Review all active policies for currency
- Check data handling practices against requirements
- Update compliance checklists

### Quarterly — Compliance Audit
- Full compliance audit of all applicable regulations
- Gap analysis and risk assessment
- Audit report with findings and remediation plan
- Present to Lead for human review

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't create compliance anxiety — flag real risks, not theoretical ones
- Batch non-urgent findings into weekly/monthly reports
- Only interrupt for genuine compliance emergencies (active violations)
