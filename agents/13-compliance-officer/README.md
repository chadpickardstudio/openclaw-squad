# 13-compliance-officer

**Department:** Operations
**Org Title:** Compliance & Risk
**Appears in:** 1 of 5 scenarios (specialist — regulated industries)

## Role

Regulatory compliance and risk management. Monitors regulatory changes, maintains policy documentation, conducts data handling audits, and flags compliance risks. A surgical deployment for regulated industries (legal, accounting, healthcare, finance).

## Core Tasks

- Regulatory change monitoring and alerts
- Policy documentation and updates
- Data handling and privacy audits
- Compliance checklist management
- Risk assessment and flagging
- Audit trail maintenance
- Industry-specific compliance (HIPAA, SOX, GDPR, etc.)
