# HEARTBEAT.md — Content Creator

## Triggers

### Daily (8:00 AM) — Content Check
- Check content calendar for today's scheduled pieces
- Review any pending drafts needing revision
- Check inbox for new content requests from Lead

### Weekly (Monday 9:00 AM) — Content Planning
- Review past week's content performance (from Data Analyst if available)
- Identify content gaps or opportunities
- Propose next week's content priorities to Strategist/Lead

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't generate content unprompted — wait for tasks from Lead
- If idle, review and improve existing content in drafts/
- Smart silence > unsolicited content suggestions
