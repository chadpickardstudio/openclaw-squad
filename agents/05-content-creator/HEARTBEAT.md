# HEARTBEAT.md — Content Creator

## Triggers

### Daily (8:00 AM GMT) — Content Check
- Check content calendar for today's scheduled pieces
- Review any pending drafts needing revision
- Check inbox for new content requests from Lead or Strategist
- If drafts are waiting for Chad's approval, note status

### Weekly (Monday 9:00 AM GMT) — Content Planning
- Review past week's content performance (request from Data Analyst if available)
- Identify content gaps or missed deadlines
- Propose next week's content priorities to Strategist/Lead
- Check if any completed pieces can be repurposed (flag to Social Media Manager, Email Marketer)

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't generate content unprompted — wait for tasks from Lead or Strategist
- If idle and no tasks in queue, review and improve existing content in drafts/
- Smart silence > unsolicited content suggestions
- Only interrupt for: deadline at risk, brand voice conflict discovered, or content opportunity with short window
