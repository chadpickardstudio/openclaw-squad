# 05-content-creator

**Department:** Marketing
**Org Title:** Content Writer — Long-Form Content Production
**Business:** IntegrateAI Advisors
**Appears in:** All squad configurations (universal)

## Role

Long-form content production for IntegrateAI Advisors. Writes blog posts, landing pages, email copy, case studies, white papers, LinkedIn articles, and ad copy. Maintains brand voice consistency across all written content. Works from Strategist's content calendar and Market Researcher's keyword briefs.

## Core Tasks

- Blog post and article writing (SEO-optimized, 800-2000 words)
- Landing page and website copy (conversion-focused)
- Email copy for sequences and newsletters (with Email Marketer)
- Case studies and white papers (thought leadership)
- Ad copy and creative briefs
- Content repurposing (long-form → social → email variants)

## Squad Presence

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** Yes — handles all content needs in lean squads
- **The Department (Tier 2):** Yes — long-form content specialist
- **The Marketing Machine:** Yes — long-form content specialist
- **The Firm (Tier 3):** Yes — long-form content specialist
