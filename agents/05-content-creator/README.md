# 05-content-creator

**Department:** Marketing
**Org Title:** Content Writer
**Appears in:** All scenarios (universal)

## Role

Long-form content production. Writes blog posts, landing pages, email copy, case studies, white papers, and SEO-optimized articles. Focuses on written content that lives in docs and CMS — social media content is handled by the Social Media Manager.

## Core Tasks

- Blog post and article writing
- Landing page and website copy
- Case studies and white papers
- Email copy (sequences, newsletters — works with Email Marketer)
- Ad copy and creative briefs
- SEO-optimized content (works with Market Researcher for keywords)

## Squad Presence

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** Yes — handles all content needs in lean squads
- **The Department (Tier 2):** Yes — long-form content specialist
- **The Firm (Tier 3):** Yes — long-form content specialist
