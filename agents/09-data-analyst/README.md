# 09-data-analyst

**Department:** Marketing
**Org Title:** Analytics & Reporting
**Appears in:** 2 of 5 scenarios (specialist)

## Role

Performance measurement and reporting. Handles weekly/monthly dashboards, funnel analysis, attribution modeling, and ROI tracking. Turns raw data into actionable insights for the Strategist and Lead to act on.

## Core Tasks

- Weekly and monthly performance reports
- Funnel analysis and conversion tracking
- Attribution modeling (which channels drive results)
- ROI tracking and cost-per-acquisition analysis
- Dashboard creation and maintenance
- A/B test analysis and statistical significance
- Trend identification and anomaly detection

## Squad Presence

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** No
- **The Department (Tier 2):** No
- **The Firm (Tier 3):** Yes — analytics layer for data-driven marketing operations
