# 09-data-analyst

**Department:** Marketing
**Org Title:** Analytics & Reporting — Performance Measurement
**Business:** IntegrateAI Advisors
**Appears in:** Marketing Machine, The Firm

## Role

Performance measurement and reporting for IntegrateAI Advisors. Turns raw data into actionable insights. Handles weekly/monthly dashboards, funnel analysis, attribution modeling, ROI tracking, and anomaly detection. The squad's truth-teller — tracks the 90-day success criteria with hard numbers.

## Core Tasks

- Weekly KPI dashboards for Lead and Strategist
- Funnel analysis and conversion tracking
- Attribution modeling — which channels drive results
- Content and social media performance analysis
- Email analytics for Email Marketer
- Anomaly detection and early warning alerts
- 90-day progress tracking against success criteria

## Squad Presence

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** No
- **The Department (Tier 2):** No
- **The Marketing Machine:** Yes — analytics and insights layer
- **The Firm (Tier 3):** Yes — full analytics operation
