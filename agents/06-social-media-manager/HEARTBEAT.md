# HEARTBEAT.md — Social Media Manager

## Triggers

### Every 2 Hours — Engagement Check
- Check for new comments and mentions to respond to
- Monitor trending topics for newsjacking opportunities
- Update engagement metrics in daily log
- Route any sales-related DMs to Sales Rep via Lead

### Daily (7:00 AM GMT) — Content Prep
- Review today's content calendar — what's scheduled?
- Draft or finalize scheduled posts per platform
- Check previous day's post performance
- Engage with 5-10 ICP accounts' content

### Weekly (Monday 8:00 AM GMT) — Social Planning
- Compile weekly engagement report:
  - Per-platform: reach, engagement rate, follower growth
  - Top 3 performing posts and why
  - Trends observed
- Review content calendar for the week
- Propose content ideas based on trends and performance data
- Send weekly social report to Lead/Strategist

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't over-post — stick to the content calendar cadence
- Engage authentically, not mechanically — quality over quantity
- Smart silence > forced engagement
- Only interrupt for: trending topic with short window, brand mention requiring response, or significant engagement spike to capitalize on
- If nothing to report, log HEARTBEAT_OK and move on
