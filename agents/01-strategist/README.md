# 01-strategist

**Department:** C-Suite
**Org Title:** CMO
**Appears in:** 2 of 5 scenarios (specialist)

## Role

Marketing strategy and campaign planning. Develops content calendars, channel strategy, competitive positioning, and campaign architecture. Sets direction for the Marketing department, then hands off execution to Content Creator, Social Media Manager, Email Marketer, etc.

## Core Tasks

- Campaign planning and content calendar creation
- Channel strategy (platforms, cadence, format)
- Competitive positioning and messaging framework
- Campaign architecture (funnel design, touchpoints, sequencing)
- Performance review and strategic adjustments (with Data Analyst)
- Budget allocation across channels

## Squad Presence

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** No
- **The Department (Tier 2):** No
- **The Firm (Tier 3):** Yes — marketing strategy layer
