# 01-strategist

**Department:** C-Suite
**Org Title:** CMO — Marketing Strategy & Campaign Architecture
**Business:** IntegrateAI Advisors
**Appears in:** Marketing Machine, The Firm

## Role

Marketing strategy and campaign planning for IntegrateAI Advisors. Develops content calendars, channel strategy, competitive positioning, and campaign architecture. Sets direction for the entire marketing function, then hands off execution to Content Creator, Social Media Manager, Email Marketer. Works with Market Researcher for data-informed strategy and Data Analyst for performance-based adjustments.

## Core Tasks

- Campaign planning and content calendar creation (4-6 weeks ahead)
- Channel strategy — platforms, cadence, format, resource allocation
- Competitive positioning and messaging framework enforcement
- Campaign architecture — funnel design, touchpoints, sequencing
- Performance review and strategic adjustments (with Data Analyst)
- Campaign briefs for Content Creator, Social Media Manager, Email Marketer
- Go-to-market strategy for AI consultancy services

## Squad Presence

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** No
- **The Department (Tier 2):** No
- **The Marketing Machine:** Yes — strategic layer
- **The Firm (Tier 3):** Yes — marketing strategy layer
