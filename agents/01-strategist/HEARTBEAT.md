# HEARTBEAT.md — Strategist

## Triggers

### Weekly (Monday 8:00 AM GMT) — Strategic Review
- Review content calendar for the week — any gaps or missed deadlines?
- Check campaign performance from Data Analyst reports
- Adjust priorities based on results and market signals
- Brief Content Creator, Social Media Manager, Email Marketer for the week's priorities
- Surface any strategic decisions needed to Lead

### Biweekly (1st and 15th) — Campaign Health Check
- Assess all active campaigns against goals
- Kill or adjust underperforming campaigns — don't let them limp
- Propose new campaigns for identified opportunities
- Review competitive positioning with Market Researcher data
- Send strategic update to Lead

### Monthly (1st of month) — Strategic Planning
- Full content calendar review and next month planning
- Campaign performance retrospective — what worked, what didn't, why
- Competitive positioning check (cross-reference Market Researcher intel)
- Channel mix review — reallocate resources to highest-performing channels
- Review progress against 90-day success criteria
- Send monthly strategy report to Lead

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't micro-brief agents — set direction and deadlines, let them execute
- Batch strategic adjustments into weekly reviews — don't change course daily
- Only interrupt for: urgent competitive moves, major performance shifts, or time-sensitive opportunities
- If nothing changed since last review, log HEARTBEAT_OK and move on
- Strategy is a loop — review cadence matters more than individual reviews
