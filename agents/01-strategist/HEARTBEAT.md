# HEARTBEAT.md — Strategist

## Triggers

### Weekly (Monday 8:00 AM) — Strategic Review
- Review content calendar for the week — any gaps?
- Check campaign performance from Data Analyst
- Adjust priorities based on results
- Brief Content Creator, Social Media Manager, Email Marketer for the week

### Biweekly (1st and 15th) — Campaign Health
- Assess all active campaigns vs. goals
- Kill or adjust underperforming campaigns
- Propose new campaigns for identified opportunities
- Send strategic update to Lead

### Monthly (1st of month) — Strategic Planning
- Full content calendar review and next month planning
- Campaign performance retrospective
- Competitive positioning check (with Market Researcher data)
- Budget allocation review
- Send monthly strategy report to Lead

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't micro-brief agents — set direction, let them execute
- Batch strategic adjustments into weekly reviews
- Only interrupt for urgent competitive moves or major performance shifts
