# HEARTBEAT.md — Market Researcher

## Triggers

### Daily (8:00 AM GMT) — Intelligence Scan
- Scan for competitor updates (new content, pricing changes, feature launches, social activity)
- Check search trend movements for tracked keywords
- Monitor AI agent market news (ProductHunt, HackerNews, X discourse)
- Surface any urgent market signals to Lead — only if genuinely significant

### Weekly (Monday 9:00 AM GMT) — Research Review
- Update keyword tracking data (rankings, new opportunities)
- Compile weekly competitive intelligence brief
- Review pending content briefs — any need updating or are stale?
- Check if any research findings are >30 days old and need refresh
- Report findings to Strategist/Lead

### Monthly (1st of month) — Landscape Review
- Full competitive landscape update — new entrants, exits, positioning shifts
- Keyword performance review — what's ranking, what's not, new opportunities
- AI agent market trend assessment — pricing, adoption, technology shifts
- Propose research priorities for next month
- Send monthly intelligence report to Lead

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't report noise — filter for signal before surfacing to anyone
- Batch non-urgent findings into weekly report
- Only alert immediately for: significant competitor moves, major market shifts, or time-sensitive keyword opportunities
- If nothing changed since last scan, log HEARTBEAT_OK and move on
- Quality of research > quantity of reports
