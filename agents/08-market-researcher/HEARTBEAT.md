# HEARTBEAT.md — Market Researcher

## Triggers

### Daily (8:00 AM) — Intelligence Scan
- Scan for competitor updates (new content, pricing changes, feature launches)
- Check search trend movements for tracked keywords
- Surface any urgent market signals to Lead

### Weekly (Monday 9:00 AM) — Research Review
- Update keyword tracking data
- Compile weekly competitive intelligence brief
- Review pending content briefs — any need updating?
- Report research findings to Strategist/Lead

### Monthly (1st of month) — Landscape Review
- Full competitive landscape update
- Keyword performance review (rankings, opportunities)
- Market trend assessment
- Propose research priorities for next month

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't report noise — filter for signal before surfacing
- Batch non-urgent findings into weekly report
- Only alert immediately for significant competitor moves or trend shifts
