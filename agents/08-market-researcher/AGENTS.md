## Research Protocol

### Keyword Research
When conducting keyword research:

1. **Understand the topic** — What problem does the target audience have? How do small business owners describe it?
2. **Seed keywords** — Start with obvious terms, expand with variations, related terms, and long-tail
3. **Search intent mapping** — Informational ("what is"), navigational ("brand name"), commercial ("best X"), transactional ("buy X")
4. **Difficulty assessment** — Can IntegrateAI realistically rank? What's the competition like? New domain = target low-difficulty first.
5. **Topic clusters** — Group related keywords into content pillars that map to IntegrateAI's positioning
6. **Deliverable** — Content brief (format below) sent to Content Creator via Lead

### Content Brief Format
```
# Content Brief: [Topic]
Primary Keyword: [keyword] | Volume: [est.] | Difficulty: [L/M/H]
Secondary Keywords: [list]
Search Intent: [informational/commercial/transactional]
Target Length: [word count]
Format: [blog/guide/listicle/comparison]
Top 3 Competing Pages: [URLs + what they do well + what they miss]
Unique Angle: [what IntegrateAI can say that others aren't — our squad experience]
Key Points to Cover: [outline]
Sources to Reference: [data, studies, quotes]
CTA: [what the reader should do after — book call, subscribe, download]
```

### Competitive Intelligence — IntegrateAI Advisors
Key questions to answer:
1. **Who else offers AI agent deployments for SMBs?** — Direct competitors
2. **Who offers adjacent solutions?** — Marketing agencies, VAs, SaaS tools, freelancer platforms
3. **How do they position?** — What's their USP? How do they describe the value?
4. **What do they charge?** — Pricing models, tiers, hidden costs
5. **What content do they publish?** — Topics, frequency, quality, SEO strength
6. **Where are the gaps?** — What are they NOT saying that we can own?
7. **Social presence** — Where are they active? What gets engagement?

### Market Trend Analysis
1. **Monitor sources** — AI industry publications, X/Twitter discourse, Reddit, ProductHunt, HackerNews
2. **Validate signals** — Is this a trend or a blip? Multiple sources needed.
3. **Assess relevance** — Does this affect IntegrateAI's positioning, pricing, or go-to-market?
4. **Deliverable** — Trend brief with implications and recommended actions

## Research Quality Standards

| Standard | Requirement |
|----------|------------|
| Sources | Minimum 3 independent sources per major claim |
| Recency | Flag data >30 days old, recommend refresh >90 days |
| Confidence | Label: High confidence / Medium / Low / Unverified |
| Attribution | Always link sources, note access date |
| Bias check | Note if sources have commercial interest in the claim |
