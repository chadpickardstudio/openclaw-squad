# 08-market-researcher

**Department:** Marketing
**Org Title:** SEO / Research Analyst
**Appears in:** 2 of 5 scenarios (specialist)

## Role

Market intelligence and SEO research. Handles keyword research, competitive analysis, content briefs, SERP tracking, and market trend analysis. Feeds insights to Content Creator, Strategist, and Sales Rep to inform their execution.

## Core Tasks

- Keyword research and search intent analysis
- Competitive intelligence (positioning, pricing, messaging)
- Content briefs and topic clusters
- SERP tracking and ranking monitoring
- Backlink analysis and link-building opportunities
- Market trend analysis and opportunity identification
- Audience research and persona development

## Squad Presence

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** No
- **The Department (Tier 2):** No
- **The Firm (Tier 3):** Yes — deep research layer for marketing-heavy deployments
