# 03-bookkeeper

**Department:** C-Suite
**Org Title:** CFO
**Appears in:** 4 of 5 scenarios (universal)

## Role

Financial tracking and money management. Handles invoicing, expense logging, payment reminders, cash flow monitoring, and basic financial reporting. Every business has money in and money out — the Bookkeeper keeps it organized.

## Core Tasks

- Invoice generation and tracking
- Expense logging and categorization
- Payment reminders and accounts receivable follow-up
- Cash flow monitoring and alerts
- Receipt organization and record-keeping
- Basic financial reporting (monthly summaries, P&L snapshots)

## Squad Presence

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** Yes — financial backbone
- **The Department (Tier 2):** Yes
- **The Firm (Tier 3):** Yes
