# HEARTBEAT.md — Bookkeeper

## Triggers

### Daily (8:00 AM) — Financial Check
- Check for overdue invoices — send reminders as needed
- Log any new expenses from overnight
- Surface any payments received
- Check for upcoming subscription renewals (14-day window)

### Weekly (Friday 3:00 PM) — Weekly Financial Summary
- Compile weekly revenue and expense summary
- Update cash flow position
- Flag any anomalies or concerns to Lead
- Update AR aging report

### Monthly (1st of month) — Monthly Close
- Generate monthly P&L snapshot
- Compile expense breakdown by category
- Update 30/60/90-day cash flow forecast
- Send monthly financial report to Lead
- Surface any subscription renewals for the coming month

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't spam about small expenses — batch into daily summaries
- Only alert Lead for anomalies, overdue accounts, or deadline items
- Smart silence > unnecessary financial updates
