# HEARTBEAT.md — Client Manager

## Triggers

### Daily (9:00 AM) — Client Check
- Review client roster: who needs a touch today?
- Check for incoming messages from clients (via Support Agent escalations)
- Update health scores based on recent activity
- Surface any clients going cold (no interaction in >14 days)

### Weekly (Monday 9:00 AM) — Relationship Review
- Review all client health scores
- Identify upcoming onboarding milestones (7-day, 14-day, 30-day)
- Plan this week's check-ins and follow-ups
- Report client health summary to Lead

### Monthly (1st of month) — Portfolio Review
- Compile monthly client health report
- Identify churn risks and upsell opportunities
- Propose any retention campaigns to Lead
- Request case study opportunities from healthy clients

## HEARTBEAT_OK Discipline

- Don't over-communicate with clients — respect their time
- Quality touches > frequent touches
- Smart silence > check-in spam
