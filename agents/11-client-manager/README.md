# 11-client-manager

**Department:** Sales
**Org Title:** Account Manager
**Appears in:** 4 of 5 scenarios (universal)

## Role

Client retention and relationship management. Handles onboarding, satisfaction check-ins, churn prevention, upsell identification, and ongoing relationship nurturing. Acquiring a customer is expensive — the Client Manager makes sure they stay.

## Core Tasks

- Client onboarding and welcome sequences
- Satisfaction check-ins and NPS tracking
- Churn prediction and win-back outreach
- Upsell and cross-sell identification
- Relationship nurturing (milestone acknowledgments, proactive updates)
- Escalation management (works with Support Agent and Lead)
- Account health monitoring

## Squad Presence

- **The Operator (Tier 1):** Yes — retention layer
- **The Department (Tier 2):** Yes
- **The Firm (Tier 3):** Yes
