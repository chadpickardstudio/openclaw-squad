## Geography

**Base:** Milton under Wychwood, Oxfordshire
**Radius:** 50 miles
**Why local first:** Face-to-face discovery calls, word-of-mouth referrals, local networking, easier trust-building. Expand nationally after proving the model locally.

### Towns & Cities in Range

**Core (0-15 miles) — Primary targets:**
- Witney, Chipping Norton, Burford, Carterton, Charlbury
- Woodstock, Eynsham, Stow-on-the-Wold, Moreton-in-Marsh

**Mid-range (15-30 miles):**
- Oxford, Banbury, Bicester, Cirencester, Cheltenham
- Abingdon, Didcot, Faringdon, Stroud

**Outer (30-50 miles):**
- Reading, Swindon, Gloucester, Stratford-upon-Avon
- Leamington Spa, Warwick, High Wycombe, Aylesbury, Newbury

### Prospecting Priority

1. **Start with Core** — businesses you can meet in person within 20 minutes
2. **Expand to Mid-range** — still easy to meet, larger business density (Oxford, Cheltenham, Banbury)
3. **Outer ring** — video calls acceptable, target only when pipeline needs filling

## Priority Verticals

Ranked by density in the Cotswolds/Oxfordshire area and fit with our offering:

### Tier 1 — Best Fit (high density, clear pain, low marketing maturity)

| Vertical | Why | Example Pain Points |
|----------|-----|-------------------|
| **Hospitality** (pubs, restaurants, cafés, B&Bs) | Cotswolds is saturated. Owners run everything. No marketing team. | "I know I should post on social media but I have no time." "We rely entirely on word of mouth." |
| **Trades** (builders, plumbers, electricians, landscapers) | Huge demand, zero marketing effort. Most run on reputation alone. | "All my work comes from recommendations." "I've got a website but it does nothing." |
| **Health & wellness** (gyms, PTs, clinics, physios, dentists) | Competitive locally, most have basic web presence but no strategy. | "We get clients through Google but I don't know why." "I tried social media but gave up." |

### Tier 2 — Good Fit (good density, more sophisticated but still underserved)

| Vertical | Why | Example Pain Points |
|----------|-----|-------------------|
| **Professional services** (accountants, solicitors, IFAs, estate agents) | Established businesses, some budget, but marketing is an afterthought. | "We've used agencies before but it was expensive and we didn't see results." |
| **Retail** (independent shops, farm shops, boutiques) | Cotswolds has many. Most rely on footfall, not digital. | "We have an Instagram but nobody runs it properly." |

### Tier 3 — Opportunistic

| Vertical | Why |
|----------|-----|
| **Tourism** (activity providers, holiday lets, tour operators) | Seasonal but strong in the Cotswolds. |
| **Education** (tutoring, training providers) | Growing market, AI-curious. |
| **Property** (lettings agencies, property management) | Needs consistent marketing, often outsources badly. |

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Business size** | 1-10 employees (owner-operated or small team) |
| **Revenue** | £50k-£1M annually |
| **Location** | Within 50 miles of Milton under Wychwood |
| **Marketing maturity** | Low — doing it themselves or not at all |
| **Tech comfort** | Basic — uses email, maybe social media, possibly a website |
| **Pain** | "I know I need to do more marketing but I have no time/skills/team" |
| **Budget** | Can afford £200-500/month for a marketing solution |
| **Decision maker** | The owner. One person decides. Short sales cycle. |

## Go-to-Market Approach

### Phase 1: Warm Network (Month 1-2)
- Chad & Paul's personal networks in the area
- Local business groups (Facebook, LinkedIn)
- West Oxfordshire Chamber of Commerce
- Ask for introductions — "Do you know a local business owner who's drowning in marketing?"

### Phase 2: Local Outreach (Month 2-4)
- Sales Rep identifies 50-100 local businesses per week
- Personalised outreach via LinkedIn and email
- Content focused on local case studies and local SEO keywords
- Google Business Profile optimised and active

### Phase 3: Inbound (Month 3+)
- Blog content ranking for local keywords
- Newsletter building via Beehiiv
- Social proof from first 2-3 clients
- Referral programme — happy clients introduce others
