## Key Decisions & Rationale

### Beehiiv over Ghost
- Ghost Starter costs £9/mo, limits to 500 members
- Beehiiv Launch is free, supports 2,500 subscribers, includes blog + newsletter + custom domain
- Saves £108/year with more features

### Discord over Slack
- Slack Free limits to 10 integrations (squad needs 8+ agent bots plus standard tools)
- Slack Free has 90-day message history limit
- Discord: unlimited messages, unlimited bots, voice channels, all free
- Revisit Slack Pro when revenue supports £7.25/user/mo

### No Framer
- Website already exists on Vercel (free)
- Framer Mini would cost £4/mo for no additional benefit
- Saves £48/year

### Wave over Stripe for invoicing
- Stripe is pay-per-transaction (already great for payments)
- Wave adds proper invoicing UI, expense tracking, and bookkeeping features for free
- Use both: Wave for invoicing, Stripe for payment processing when needed

### Make.com over Zapier
- Zapier free: 100 tasks/mo, 5 zaps — essentially unusable
- Make.com free: 1,000 operations/mo — enough for launch automations
- Saves £20/mo vs Zapier Starter

## Upgrade Triggers

| Trigger | Action | Cost Impact |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| Hit 2,500 Beehiiv subscribers | Upgrade to Beehiiv Scale or switch to Kit | +£40/mo |
| Need 5+ social channels | Upgrade Buffer to Essentials | +£25/mo |
| Need background remover / brand kit | Upgrade Canva to Pro | +£12/mo |
| Hit 1,000 HubSpot contacts | Upgrade HubSpot to Starter | +£17/mo |
| 1,000 Make.com ops not enough | Upgrade Make.com Core or self-host n8n | +£11/mo |
| Clients expect Slack | Add Slack Pro | +£7.25/user/mo |

Don't upgrade until you hit the trigger. Free tiers are more than enough at launch.
