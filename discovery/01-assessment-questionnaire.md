# Client Assessment Questionnaire

> **OpenClaw Squad · Discovery Phase**
> The structured conversation guide for the initial client meeting
> Cross-references: Doc 22 (Agent Roster), Doc 23 (Deployment Playbook), Doc 24 (Skills Blueprint)

---

## How to Use This Questionnaire

This is a **conversation guide**, not a form. The goal is a 15–20 minute conversation that captures everything needed to deploy the right squad. Every question maps to a specific field in the deployment specification.

**Who runs this:** Claude Code (via the Lead agent during automated onboarding) or the founder (Chad) during a live discovery call.

**Output:** A completed assessment that feeds into the Assessment-to-Squad Router (see `discovery/02-router.md`).

**Rules:**
- Don't skip sections. Missing data = missing agent capabilities.
- Record answers verbatim where possible — the client's language reveals their brand voice.
- If a question doesn't apply, mark it "N/A" and move on.
- Total time: 15–20 minutes. Respect the founder's time.

## Section 8 — Closing (1 minute)

### 8.1 Recap

Read back a summary of what they said:

*"Here's what I heard: You run [business] in [industry]. Your biggest pain is [pain point]. You use [tools]. You want help with [functions]. Your budget is around [amount]. Success looks like [criteria]. Does that sound right?"*

### 8.2 Preview

Based on the assessment, preview the recommendation:

*"Based on what you've told me, I'm recommending [The Operator / The Department / The Marketing Machine] — that's [N] AI agents covering [functions]. Your estimated monthly cost will be [range]. I'll have the deployment plan ready for your review within [timeframe]."*
