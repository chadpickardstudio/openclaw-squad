# The Marketing Machine — Tier 3 Squad

**Agents:** 7 (Lead + full Marketing dept + Sales Rep)
**For:** Businesses whose primary pain is marketing — agencies, DTC brands, content-driven businesses
**Monthly cost:** Premium tier
**Value prop:** "A full marketing department for the price of one freelancer."

## What It Does

A complete digital marketing operation:
- **Lead (CEO):** Orchestrates campaigns, synthesizes results
- **Content Creator:** Blog posts, landing pages, email copy, case studies
- **Social Media Manager:** Platform-native content, engagement, community
- **Email Marketer:** Sequences, segmentation, A/B testing, newsletters
- **Market Researcher:** Keywords, competitive intel, content briefs, SERP tracking
- **Data Analyst:** Performance reporting, attribution, ROI tracking, dashboards
- **Sales Rep:** Lead gen, outreach, pipeline (turns marketing into revenue)

## Who It's For

- Businesses spending $2k+/month on freelancers or agencies with mediocre results
- Companies that need a full marketing department but can't hire one
- Content-driven businesses that need consistent, multi-channel output
- Agencies that want to offer AI-powered marketing as a service

## What It Doesn't Do

- No financial tracking (add Bookkeeper separately)
- No operational processes (add Ops Manager separately)
- No frontline customer support (add Support Agent separately)
- No client retention management (add Client Manager — or use Operator as base)

## Deployment

1. Copy `openclaw.json5` to `~/.openclaw/openclaw.json`
2. Copy agent workspace files from `agents/` for all 7 agents
3. Copy `shared/` contents to `~/.openclaw/workspace-shared/`
4. Customize `USER.md` in Lead's workspace for the client
5. Run `openclaw gateway` to start
