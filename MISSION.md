We don't sell software. We don't sell subscriptions to a dashboard. We deploy a workforce — a team of AI agents that operate like human employees, running inside the founder's own infrastructure, connected to their own tools, working 24/7.

## What We Do

We deploy a team of autonomous AI agents — built on OpenClaw — that take over entire business functions. Not a chatbot. Not an assistant. A team.

A marketing team that creates content, manages social media, writes copy, analyses performance, and adjusts strategy — without being told. A sales pipeline that researches prospects, writes outreach, follows up, and books calls. A support team that handles customer enquiries, escalates issues, and maintains a knowledge base.

Each agent has a defined role, personality, skills, and operating rhythm. They communicate with each other through a Lead agent (the CEO of the squad) who routes work, resolves conflicts, and ensures quality. The founder talks to the Lead. The Lead manages the team.

## How It Works

### 1. Discovery
We meet the founder. We learn their business, their pain points, what functions they need covered, and what tools they already use (Slack, email, CRM, social accounts, etc.).

### 2. Team Selection
We select agents from our **universal agent library** — a pre-built collection of specialist roles, each backed by deep research on how to build the most effective, secure, and cost-efficient autonomous agents possible. The library includes:

- **Lead** — The CEO of the squad. Routes work, manages agents, enforces quality.
- **Content Creator** — Social media, copywriting, visual content, brand voice.
- **Market Researcher** — Competitor analysis, trend monitoring, audience insights.
- **Sales Rep** — Prospecting, outreach, follow-up, pipeline management.
- **Client Manager** — Onboarding, retention, relationship management.
- **Support Agent** — Tier 1/2 customer support, FAQ maintenance, escalation.
- **Strategist** — Business strategy, campaign planning, growth roadmaps.
- **Data Analyst** — Reporting, dashboards, performance analysis, insights.
- **Bookkeeper** — Invoicing, expenses, financial tracking, reconciliation.
- **Ops Manager** — Process optimisation, vendor management, logistics.
- **Recruiter** — Talent sourcing, screening, interview coordination.
- **Compliance Officer** — Regulatory checks, data privacy, legal guardrails.
- **Engineer** — Technical builds, integrations, automation scripts.
- **Designer** — UI/UX, visual assets, brand materials.
- **System Architect** — Infrastructure design, security hardening, scaling.

A typical small business squad is 1 Lead + 4-6 specialists. We pick the right combination for the founder's specific needs.

### 3. Configuration
We configure each agent with:
- A **SOUL.md** — their personality, values, and behavioural core
- An **IDENTITY.md** — their role, responsibilities, and reporting line
- A **GOALS.md** — their objectives and success metrics
- A **CONSTRAINTS.md** — their boundaries and safety rules
- A **STYLE.md** — their communication tone and preferences
- A **HEARTBEAT.md** — their operational rhythm (when to check in, when to act proactively)
- An **EVOLUTION.md** — how they grow, learn, and earn more capabilities over time
- A **skills.md** — their tools and access tiers (starting minimal, earning more)

Every agent starts with restricted tool access (Tier 1). The Lead grants additional capabilities as the agent demonstrates competence — exactly like onboarding a new employee.

### 4. Deployment
We spin up the squad on the founder's infrastructure (or ours), connect it to their communication channels (Slack, WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord), wire up their tools (CRM, social accounts, email, analytics), and hand them a working team.

### 5. Ongoing
The squad runs autonomously. The Lead manages day-to-day operations. Agents evolve through self-reflection loops, earning new tools and capabilities. We provide oversight, tuning, and scaling as the business grows.

## Why It Works

This system is built on 21 deep research documents covering every aspect of multi-agent AI squad architecture:

- **Agent isolation** — Each agent has its own workspace, memory, and skills. No cross-contamination, no "hive mind" drift.
- **Lead-as-CEO model** — One orchestrator per squad. No middle-management overhead. Dynamic task routing, not hardcoded pipelines.
- **Hybrid model routing** — Premium AI models for the Lead's decision-making, cost-effective local models for worker agents. Full squads run for under $10/month.
- **Security hardening** — Docker sandboxing, tool allow/deny lists, encrypted secrets, prompt injection prevention, audit trails.
- **Memory hygiene** — Per-agent memory with daily logs, semantic indexing, and automatic compaction. Agents remember what matters and forget what doesn't.
- **Agent evolution** — Structured self-improvement loops. Agents critique their own work, propose improvements, and grow over time.
- **Model agnosticism** — Swap underlying AI models without rewriting prompts or skills. Future-proof by design.
- **Backup and rollback** — Git-based snapshots, encrypted archives, disaster recovery. AI memory treated like a production database.
- **Legal compliance** — GDPR, CCPA, and data privacy guardrails built into agent configuration, not bolted on after.
- **Scalability** — Proven patterns for 5-19 agent squads with clear ceilings and burnout prevention.

This isn't a prototype. The research is done. The patterns are production-tested. We're building on the strongest foundation available.
