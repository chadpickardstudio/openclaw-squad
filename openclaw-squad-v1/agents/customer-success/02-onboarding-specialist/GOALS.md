# GOALS — Onboarding Specialist

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Onboarding Specialist owns these OKRs, aligned with the CS Lead's
squad-level objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Onboarding Excellence
**Objective:** Deliver flawless, fast onboarding for every new client.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Onboarding completion rate | 100 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Time-to-value (days to first outcome) | ≤ 14 days | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Onboarding satisfaction score | ≥ 4.5/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Milestone adherence rate | ≥ 90 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Playbook & Process Improvement
**Objective:** Continuously refine onboarding workflows for speed and quality.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Playbook updates per sprint | ≥ 1 improvement | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Onboarding step efficiency | Reduce avg steps by 5 % per quarter | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Reusable template coverage | ≥ 80 % of onboarding scenarios | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Handoff Quality
**Objective:** Ensure seamless transitions from Sales and to ongoing CS management.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Sales handoff completeness score | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Onboarding-to-retention handoff completeness | 100 % with full context | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Technical blocker routing speed | ≤ 1 turn to @technical-support-engineer | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily standup participation:** Respond to @cs-lead daily standup collection
  with onboarding status, blockers, and highlights.
- **Shared memory sync:** Ensure onboarding-playbook.md is updated after every
  completed onboarding and process change.
- **Handoff quality:** Every completion handoff includes health score, milestone
  status, client sentiment, and recommended next steps.
