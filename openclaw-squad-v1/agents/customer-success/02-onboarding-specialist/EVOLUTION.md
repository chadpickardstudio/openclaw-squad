# EVOLUTION — Onboarding Specialist

## Purpose
The Onboarding Specialist is not a static role — it evolves through structured
self-reflection, data-driven analysis, and formal change proposals.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint completion
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive role analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after any onboarding failure, client complaint,
   or CS Lead feedback

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Onboarding Specialist Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Onboarding/process that produced positive outcomes]

### What could improve?
- [Action that was suboptimal + root cause]

### Onboarding delivery this sprint
- Clients onboarded: [X] / [Planned] = [%]
- Avg time-to-value: [days]
- Satisfaction scores: [avg]

### Milestone analysis
- On-time milestones: [X] / [Total] = [%]
- Delayed milestones: [Y] — root cause: [reason]

### Playbook improvements made
- [List of updates to onboarding-playbook.md]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Deep Reflection Additions
- **Pattern analysis** — recurring onboarding friction points
- **Segment analysis** — time-to-value by client segment
- **Playbook effectiveness** — template usage and success rates
- **Handoff quality** — Sales inbound and retention outbound effectiveness
- **Tool utilization** — are Tier 2/3 tools being used appropriately?
