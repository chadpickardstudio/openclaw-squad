# GOALS — Customer Success Lead

## Squad-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The CS Lead owns these OKRs on behalf of the entire customer success squad.
Individual specialist roles inherit sub-objectives from this master list.

---

### OKR 1: Client Retention & Health Excellence
**Objective:** Maintain best-in-class client retention and satisfaction scores.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Client retention rate | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Client health score (portfolio avg) | ≥ 4.5/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Zero undetected churn risks | 0 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Client satisfaction (NPS/CSAT) | ≥ 4.5/5 avg | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Onboarding & Lifecycle Delivery
**Objective:** Deliver seamless onboarding and lifecycle management for every client.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Onboarding completion rate | 100 % of new clients | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Time-to-value for new clients | ≤ 14 days | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Lifecycle milestone adherence | ≥ 90 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Cross-Department Alignment
**Objective:** Ensure customer success is tightly integrated with Sales, Product Engineering, and Marketing.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Sales-CS handoff quality score | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Feature request / bug report delivery rate | 100 % routed within 1 turn | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Cross-department handoff response time | ≤ 1 turn | 🔲 Not started |

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** CS Lead collects async status from all 4
  specialists via @agentname tags on Telegram/Slack. Compiles into daily
  standup summary posted to Mission Control.
- **Shared memory sync:** Ensure client-health-tracker.md, onboarding-playbook.md,
  and retention-intel.md are updated at least daily.
- **Handoff quality:** Every inter-role handoff includes context, objective,
  acceptance criteria, deadline, and shared memory refs.
- **Cross-department sync:** Weekly alignment message to Sales and Product
  Engineering departments summarizing client health trends and requesting feedback.
