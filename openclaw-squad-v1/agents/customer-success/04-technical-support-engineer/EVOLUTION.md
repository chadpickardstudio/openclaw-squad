# EVOLUTION — Technical Support Engineer

## Purpose
The Technical Support Engineer is not a static role — it evolves through
structured self-reflection, data-driven analysis, and formal change proposals.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint completion
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive role analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after any P0 incident, resolution failure,
   or CS Lead feedback

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Technical Support Engineer Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Resolution/process that produced positive outcomes]

### What could improve?
- [Action that was suboptimal + root cause]

### Resolution metrics this sprint
- Tickets resolved: [X] / [Total] = [%]
- Avg MTTR (P0/P1): [hours]
- Avg MTTR (P2/P3): [hours]
- Escalated to Product Eng: [Y]

### Knowledge base analysis
- New articles added: [X]
- Recurring issues resolved via KB: [Y] / [Total] = [%]

### Bug report quality
- Reports submitted: [X]
- Product Eng acceptance rate: [%]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Deep Reflection Additions
- **Pattern analysis** — recurring technical issues and systemic root causes
- **Knowledge base effectiveness** — self-service resolution rates
- **Cross-department effectiveness** — Product Engineering collaboration quality
- **Tool utilization** — are diagnostic tools being used optimally?
- **Prevention impact** — did product improvements reduce ticket volume?
