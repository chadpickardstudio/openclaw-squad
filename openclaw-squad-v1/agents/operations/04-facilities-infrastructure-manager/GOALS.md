# GOALS — Facilities & Infrastructure Manager

## Role-Level OKRs
The Facilities Manager inherits squad OKRs from the Ops Lead and owns these
infrastructure-specific objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Infrastructure Reliability
**Objective:** Maintain elite-level infrastructure uptime and reliability.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Infrastructure uptime | ≥ 99.5 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Mean time to recovery (MTTR) | ≤ 1 hour for critical systems | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Preventive maintenance completion rate | 100 % on-schedule | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Facilities Excellence
**Objective:** Provide optimal workspace and equipment support for all departments.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Maintenance request response time | ≤ 4 hours critical, ≤ 24 hours standard | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Cross-department satisfaction score | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Equipment lifecycle compliance | 100 % tracked | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Business Continuity
**Objective:** Ensure organizational resilience through comprehensive continuity planning.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Business continuity plan coverage | 100 % of critical systems | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Continuity plan test frequency | Quarterly | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Recovery time objective (RTO) compliance | 100 % within defined RTOs | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily:** Update ops-dashboard.md with infrastructure health status, tagged for relevance.
- **Per project:** Deliver infrastructure assessments to @ops-lead within
  assigned deadline, including equipment specs for @vendor-procurement.
- **Weekly:** Share maintenance schedules and capacity projections with squad
  for operational planning.
- **Cross-department:** Gather infrastructure requirements from Product Engineering
  and workspace needs from HR at least 2x per sprint.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @ops-lead daily standup collection
  with current infrastructure status and blockers.
