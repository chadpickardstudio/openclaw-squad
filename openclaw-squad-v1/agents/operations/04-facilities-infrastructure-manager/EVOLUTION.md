# EVOLUTION — Facilities & Infrastructure Manager

## Purpose
The Facilities Manager evolves through structured self-reflection and
infrastructure reliability improvement cycles.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after infrastructure outages or facility failures

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Facilities Manager Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Infrastructure achievement that drove strong outcomes]

### What could improve?
- [Gaps in monitoring, missed maintenance, slow incident response]

### Infrastructure quality this sprint
- Uptime: [%]
- Incidents responded: [X] / [Total] within SLA = [%]
- Maintenance tasks completed: [X] / [Scheduled] = [%]

### Equipment management
- Most reliable system: [system]
- Most problematic system: [system]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Infrastructure portfolio review** — are systems sized and configured optimally?
- **Incident trend analysis** — are we seeing recurring failure patterns?
- **Cross-department feedback** — are departments satisfied with infrastructure support?
