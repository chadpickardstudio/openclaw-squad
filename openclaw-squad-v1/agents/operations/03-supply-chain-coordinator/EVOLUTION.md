# EVOLUTION — Supply Chain Coordinator

## Purpose
The Supply Chain Coordinator evolves through structured self-reflection and
logistics performance improvement cycles.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after supply disruptions or fulfillment failures

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Supply Chain Coordinator Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Logistics achievement that drove strong outcomes]

### What could improve?
- [Gaps in coverage, missed disruptions, slow response]

### Fulfillment quality this sprint
- Orders fulfilled: [X] / [Total] = [%]
- On-time delivery rate: [%]
- Inventory accuracy: [%]

### Supplier performance
- Best performing supplier: [supplier]
- Underperforming supplier: [supplier]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Supplier portfolio review** — are we diversified against disruption risk?
- **Logistics cost analysis** — are we optimizing shipping and handling costs?
- **Cross-department feedback** — are departments satisfied with delivery performance?
