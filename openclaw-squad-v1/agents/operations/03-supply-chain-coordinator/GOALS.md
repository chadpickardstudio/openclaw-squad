# GOALS — Supply Chain Coordinator

## Role-Level OKRs
The Supply Chain Coordinator inherits squad OKRs from the Ops Lead and owns
these logistics-specific objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Fulfillment Excellence
**Objective:** Maintain elite-level order fulfillment and delivery performance.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 On-time fulfillment rate | ≥ 98 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Order accuracy rate | ≥ 99.5 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Delivery SLA compliance | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Inventory Optimization
**Objective:** Maintain optimal inventory levels with zero stockouts.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Inventory accuracy | ≥ 99 % stock-to-record | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Stockout incidents | 0 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Excess inventory reduction | ≤ 5 % overstock rate | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Supply Chain Resilience
**Objective:** Build and maintain a resilient, disruption-resistant supply chain.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Disruption response time | ≤ 4 hours from detection | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Contingency plan coverage | 100 % of critical suppliers | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Supplier delivery compliance | ≥ 95 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily:** Update ops-dashboard.md with logistics status, tagged for relevance.
- **Per project:** Deliver fulfillment tracking to @ops-lead within assigned
  deadline, including supplier data for @vendor-procurement.
- **Weekly:** Share supplier performance metrics with @vendor-procurement
  for contract review calibration.
- **Cross-department:** Gather demand forecasts from Sales and delivery
  timelines from Product Engineering at least 2x per sprint.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @ops-lead daily standup collection
  with current logistics status and blockers.
