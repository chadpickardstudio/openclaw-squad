# GOALS — Process Optimization Specialist

## Role-Level OKRs
The Process Specialist inherits squad OKRs from the Ops Lead and owns these
efficiency-specific objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Process Efficiency Excellence
**Objective:** Continuously improve operational workflows across the organization.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Process optimization reports delivered | ≥ 2 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Average cycle time reduction per optimized process | ≥ 15 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Process compliance audit pass rate | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Process Documentation & Playbook
**Objective:** Maintain comprehensive, current process documentation.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Process playbook freshness | ≤ 7 days old on key sections | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Documented processes coverage | 100 % of critical workflows | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Playbook actionability score (Ops Lead rating) | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Automation & Innovation
**Objective:** Identify and advance automation opportunities.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Automation candidates identified | ≥ 1 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Automation-to-implementation conversion rate | ≥ 25 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Innovation briefs delivered | ≥ 1 per month | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily:** Update process-playbook.md with any new findings, tagged for relevance.
- **Per project:** Deliver optimization report to @ops-lead within assigned
  deadline, including workflow recommendations for @supply-chain and @facilities-manager.
- **Weekly:** Share process metrics with squad for operational dashboard calibration.
- **Cross-department:** Gather workflow feedback from other departments at
  least 2x per sprint via @ops-lead routing.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @ops-lead daily standup collection
  with current optimization tasks and blockers.
