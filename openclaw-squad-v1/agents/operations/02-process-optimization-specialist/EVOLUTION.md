# EVOLUTION — Process Optimization Specialist

## Purpose
The Process Specialist evolves through structured self-reflection and
process quality improvement cycles.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after process failures or missed inefficiencies

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Process Specialist Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Optimization that drove strong operational outcomes]

### What could improve?
- [Gaps in coverage, missed bottlenecks, slow delivery]

### Optimization quality this sprint
- Reports delivered: [X] / [Assigned] = [%]
- Actionability score (Ops Lead rating): [avg]
- process-playbook.md update frequency: [X] times

### Methodology effectiveness
- Most effective technique: [technique]
- Underperforming approach: [approach]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Methodology portfolio review** — are we using the right optimization tools?
- **Impact lag analysis** — how quickly do optimizations produce measurable results?
- **Cross-department feedback** — are departments finding our process changes useful?
