# EVOLUTION — Vendor & Procurement Specialist

## Purpose
The Procurement Specialist evolves through structured self-reflection and
vendor management improvement cycles.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after vendor failures or procurement compliance issues

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Procurement Specialist Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Vendor deal or procurement efficiency that drove strong outcomes]

### What could improve?
- [Gaps in vendor coverage, missed savings, slow cycle times]

### Procurement quality this sprint
- Procurements completed: [X] / [Requested] = [%]
- Average cycle time: [days]
- vendor-registry.md update frequency: [X] times

### Vendor portfolio health
- Best performing vendor: [vendor]
- Underperforming vendor: [vendor]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Vendor portfolio review** — are we diversified and getting best value?
- **Spend trend analysis** — are costs trending in the right direction?
- **Cross-department feedback** — are departments satisfied with procurement support?
