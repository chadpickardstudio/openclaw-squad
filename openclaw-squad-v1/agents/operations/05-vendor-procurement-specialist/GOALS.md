# GOALS — Vendor & Procurement Specialist

## Role-Level OKRs
The Procurement Specialist inherits squad OKRs from the Ops Lead and owns
these vendor-management-specific objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Vendor Excellence
**Objective:** Maintain a high-performing, reliable vendor portfolio.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Vendor SLA compliance rate | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Vendor performance reviews completed | 100 % quarterly | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Vendor satisfaction with partnership | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Procurement Efficiency
**Objective:** Run lean, fast procurement workflows that deliver value.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Procurement cycle time | ≤ 5 business days standard | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Cost savings vs baseline | ≥ 10 % per quarter | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Procurement compliance rate | 100 % policy-compliant | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Contract & Registry Management
**Objective:** Maintain comprehensive, current vendor documentation.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Vendor registry freshness | Updated within 48 hours of changes | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Contract renewal on-time rate | 100 % renewed before expiry | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Registry completeness | 100 % of active vendors documented | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily:** Update vendor-registry.md with any new findings, tagged for relevance.
- **Per project:** Deliver vendor evaluations to @ops-lead within assigned
  deadline, including delivery data for @supply-chain and equipment specs
  for @facilities-manager.
- **Weekly:** Share spend analytics and vendor performance summaries with squad.
- **Cross-department:** Gather procurement requirements from Finance and
  contract guidance from Legal at least 2x per sprint.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @ops-lead daily standup collection
  with current procurement pipeline and blockers.
