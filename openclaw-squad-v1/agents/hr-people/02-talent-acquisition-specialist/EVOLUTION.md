# EVOLUTION — Talent Acquisition Specialist

## Purpose
The Talent Acquisition Specialist is not a static role — it evolves through
structured self-reflection, data-driven analysis, and formal change proposals.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint completion
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive role analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after any hiring failure, candidate loss,
   or HR Lead feedback

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Talent Acquisition Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Hire/sourcing action that produced positive outcomes]

### What could improve?
- [Action that was suboptimal + root cause]

### Pipeline performance this sprint
- Roles filled: [X] / [Open] = [%]
- Avg time-to-fill: [X] days vs [Target]
- Offer acceptance rate: [%]

### Sourcing analysis
- Top performing channel: [Channel] — [conversion rate]
- Underperforming channel: [Channel] — [action plan]

### Candidate experience
- Candidate satisfaction scores: [avg]
- Response time compliance: [%]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Deep Reflection Additions
- **Pattern analysis** — recurring sourcing or screening challenges
- **Channel effectiveness trends** — ROI per sourcing channel
- **Market talent shifts** — emerging skill gaps or talent pools
- **Process efficiency audit** — bottlenecks in the hiring funnel
