# GOALS — Talent Acquisition Specialist

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Talent Acquisition Specialist owns these OKRs, inherited from the
HR Lead's squad-level objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Pipeline Health & Hiring Velocity
**Objective:** Maintain a robust talent pipeline that fills roles quickly and with quality.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Pipeline fill rate for open roles | ≥ 90 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Time-to-fill vs target | ≤ target days per role level | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Candidate quality score (hiring manager rating) | ≥ 4/5 avg | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Offer acceptance rate | ≥ 85 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Sourcing Excellence
**Objective:** Diversify and optimize sourcing channels for maximum reach and quality.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Active sourcing channels tracked | ≥ 5 channels | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Channel conversion rate tracking | Updated monthly | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Referral pipeline contribution | ≥ 30 % of hires | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Candidate Experience
**Objective:** Deliver a world-class candidate experience throughout the hiring process.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Candidate response time | ≤ 24 hours | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Candidate experience rating | ≥ 4.5/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Interview-to-offer conversion rate | ≥ 25 % | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** Report pipeline status, blockers, and highlights
  to @hr-lead via Telegram/Slack.
- **Shared memory sync:** Update talent-pipeline.md ≥ 3x per week with
  pipeline changes, new candidates, and stage transitions.
- **Handoff quality:** Every candidate handoff to @employee-experience includes
  full candidate profile, offer details, and start date.
- **Cross-department sync:** Weekly check-in with hiring managers across
  departments to validate role requirements and hiring urgency.
