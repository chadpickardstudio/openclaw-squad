# GOALS — Learning & Development Coordinator

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The L&D Coordinator owns these OKRs, inherited from the HR Lead's
squad-level objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Training Program Effectiveness
**Objective:** Deliver high-impact training programs that measurably improve workforce capabilities.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Program completion rate | ≥ 90 % of enrolled | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Post-training skill assessment score | ≥ 4/5 avg | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Learning NPS | ≥ 50 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Business impact measurement | Tracked for 100 % of programs | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Workforce Capability Building
**Objective:** Close critical skill gaps and build future-ready workforce capabilities.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Skill gap assessments completed | ≥ 1 per department per quarter | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Employee development plan coverage | ≥ 80 % of employees | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Priority skill gap closure rate | ≥ 70 % per quarter | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Career Development & Succession
**Objective:** Enable clear career progression and ensure leadership pipeline readiness.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Career path framework coverage | 100 % of role families | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Internal promotion rate | ≥ 60 % of open leadership roles | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Succession readiness for critical roles | ≥ 2 ready-now candidates | 🔲 Not started |

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** Report training status, program updates,
  and blockers to @hr-lead via Telegram/Slack.
- **Shared memory sync:** Read engagement-tracker.md weekly for skill gap
  signals and development need inputs from @employee-experience.
- **Handoff quality:** Every skill-based progression update to @comp-benefits
  includes competency data, assessment results, and level recommendations.
- **Cross-department sync:** Quarterly training needs assessment with
  department leads to align development priorities with business goals.
