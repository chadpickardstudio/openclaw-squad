# GOALS — Employee Experience Manager

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Employee Experience Manager owns these OKRs, inherited from the
HR Lead's squad-level objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Employee Engagement Excellence
**Objective:** Achieve and maintain best-in-class employee engagement scores.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Organization-wide engagement score | ≥ 4.5/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Survey response rate | ≥ 85 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Engagement program participation | ≥ 80 % eligible employees | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Quarter-over-quarter engagement trend | Stable or improving | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Onboarding & Retention
**Objective:** Deliver world-class onboarding and minimize unwanted attrition.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Onboarding satisfaction score | ≥ 4.5/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 90-day new hire retention | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Voluntary attrition rate | ≤ industry benchmark | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Culture & Inclusion
**Objective:** Build a measurably inclusive and values-aligned workplace culture.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Culture alignment score | ≥ 4/5 (employee rating) | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Inclusion index | ≥ 4/5 across all demographics | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Recognition program utilization | ≥ 70 % of managers participating | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** Report engagement status, program updates,
  and blockers to @hr-lead via Telegram/Slack.
- **Shared memory sync:** Update engagement-tracker.md weekly with survey
  results, program metrics, and retention data.
- **Handoff quality:** Every onboarding handoff from @talent-acquisition
  includes full new hire profile, start date, and team assignment.
- **Cross-department sync:** Monthly culture check-ins with department leads
  to gather sentiment and align on engagement initiatives.
