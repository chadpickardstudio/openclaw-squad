# GOALS — Compensation & Benefits Analyst

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Compensation & Benefits Analyst owns these OKRs, inherited from the
HR Lead's squad-level objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Compensation Competitiveness & Equity
**Objective:** Maintain market-competitive, internally equitable compensation structures.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Market benchmarking freshness | Updated semi-annually | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Pay equity audit completion | 100 % of roles annually | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Compensation within target market percentile | ≥ 90 % of roles | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Pay equity disparity remediation | 100 % of flagged items | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Benefits Program Optimization
**Objective:** Deliver high-value benefits programs that maximize employee satisfaction and cost efficiency.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Benefits enrollment rate | ≥ 80 % of eligible | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Benefits satisfaction score | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Cost-per-employee trend | Stable or declining | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Financial Alignment & Budget Discipline
**Objective:** Ensure compensation programs are financially sustainable and budget-compliant.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Compensation budget adherence | ≤ 100 % of allocation | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Offer modeling turnaround time | ≤ 24 hours standard | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Budget forecast accuracy | ≥ 95 % vs actuals | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** Report compensation status, pending analyses,
  and blockers to @hr-lead via Telegram/Slack.
- **Shared memory sync:** Read talent-pipeline.md for offer pipeline needs
  and employee-handbook.md for compensation policy alignment.
- **Handoff quality:** Every offer model delivered to @hr-lead includes
  market data, internal equity check, and budget impact assessment.
- **Cross-department sync:** Monthly alignment with Finance on compensation
  budgets, headcount costs, and benefits expense forecasting.
