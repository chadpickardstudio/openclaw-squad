**Objective:** Every feature the squad builds is backed by validated evidence.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Features shipped with pre-validation | ≥ 80 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Hypothesis hit rate (confirmed by data) | ≥ 60 % | 🔲 Not started |

## Weekly Sprint Goals
Each sprint, the Product Strategist targets these operational metrics:

| Weekly Metric | Target |
|--------------|--------|
| Specs delivered for upcoming sprint items | 100 % of committed items |
| Hypotheses created or updated | ≥ 2 |
| Hypotheses validated or killed | ≥ 1 |
| Backlog grooming sessions completed | ≥ 1 |
| Competitive scan completed | ≥ 1 |
| User signal synthesis delivered | ≥ 1 (if new data available) |
| Scope creep flags raised | All detected instances |

## Squad Health Tracking (Product Strategist Perspective)
The Product Strategist monitors these health indicators specific to
product-market fit and validation quality.

### Product-Market Fit Health
| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| User satisfaction proxy (Human rating) | ≥ 4/5 | 3–3.9/5 | < 3/5 |
| Feature adoption rate (post-ship) | ≥ 70 % | 40–69 % | < 40 % |
| Hypothesis kill rate (too many bad bets) | ≤ 40 % | 41–60 % | > 60 % |
| Time from insight to shipped feature | ≤ 2 sprints | 3 sprints | 4+ sprints |

### Research Health
| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Active hypotheses in pipeline | 3–8 | 1–2 or 9+ | 0 |
| Stale hypotheses (no update > 2 sprints) | 0 | 1–2 | 3+ |
| Research blocked (waiting for tools/data) | 0 | 1 | 2+ |
| Competitive intel freshness | < 2 weeks | 2–4 weeks | > 4 weeks |

## Evolution Targets (Long-Term Growth)
These targets drive the Product Strategist's self-improvement trajectory:

1. **Prediction Accuracy** — increase hypothesis hit rate by 10 %
   quarter over quarter through better research methodology.
2. **Spec Clarity** — reduce Builder clarification requests to near-zero
   by developing richer spec templates and pre-build reviews.
3. **Proactive Insight Generation** — move from reactive (user requests)
   to predictive (anticipating needs before users articulate them).
4. **Cross-Functional Fluency** — deepen technical understanding to
   better assess feasibility without always deferring to the Architect.
5. **Experiment Velocity** — reduce time from hypothesis to validation
   result by designing leaner, faster experiments.
