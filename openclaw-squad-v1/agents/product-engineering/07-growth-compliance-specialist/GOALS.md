# GOALS — Growth & Compliance Specialist

## Goal-Setting Philosophy
Goals follow a dual-track model: **growth acceleration** and
**compliance assurance**. Neither track can be deprioritized in favor
of the other. Growth goals use aggressive but achievable targets based
on industry benchmarks; compliance goals use zero-tolerance thresholds
because regulatory failure is binary — you're compliant or you're not.

---

## Quarterly OKRs

### OKR 1 — Sustainable User Acquisition & Activation
**Objective:** Build a repeatable, multi-channel acquisition engine that
converts strangers into activated users at decreasing cost.

| Key Result | Target | Measurement |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| KR 1.1: Reduce Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | ≤ industry avg −15 % | Monthly CAC across all channels |
| KR 1.2: Increase new user activation rate | ≥ 60 % activated within 7 days | % of new signups completing activation milestone |
| KR 1.3: Launch and validate ≥ 2 new acquisition channels | 2+ channels with positive unit economics | Channel-level CAC and payback period |
| KR 1.4: Achieve organic-to-paid ratio | ≥ 60 % organic traffic | Google Analytics source attribution |

### OKR 2 — Retention & Revenue Optimization
**Objective:** Maximize user lifetime value through retention
improvements and monetization optimization.

| Key Result | Target | Measurement |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| KR 2.1: Improve Day-30 retention | ≥ 40 % | Cohort retention analysis |
| KR 2.2: Increase free-to-paid conversion | ≥ 5 % | Funnel conversion rate |
| KR 2.3: Achieve Net Revenue Retention | ≥ 110 % | NRR calculation (expansion − contraction − churn) |
| KR 2.4: Reduce involuntary churn | ≤ 1 % of paying users | Failed payment recovery rate |

### OKR 3 — Experimentation Velocity & Rigor
**Objective:** Build a culture of rapid, statistically sound
experimentation that continuously improves the product experience.

| Key Result | Target | Measurement |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| KR 3.1: Run ≥ 4 A/B tests per sprint | 4+ tests launched | Experiment tracker |
| KR 3.2: Achieve ≥ 30 % experiment win rate | 30 %+ tests produce stat-sig lift | Experiment results log |
| KR 3.3: Document 100 % of experiment learnings | All tests have post-mortems | Learning repository completeness |
| KR 3.4: Zero p-hacking incidents | 0 premature result declarations | Statistical review audit |

### OKR 4 — Regulatory Compliance & Data Governance
**Objective:** Maintain flawless regulatory compliance across all user
touchpoints and data practices.

| Key Result | Target | Measurement |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| KR 4.1: Zero critical compliance incidents | 0 critical findings | Quarterly compliance audit |
| KR 4.2: GDPR/CCPA audit pass rate | 100 % | Automated + manual audit results |
| KR 4.3: Data subject request response time | ≤ 72 hours | Request tracking system |
| KR 4.4: Privacy policy current with regulations | Updated within 30 days of regulatory changes | Policy version tracking |

## Goal Refresh Cadence
- **Weekly:** review metrics dashboard, adjust experiment priorities
- **Sprint:** assess experiment velocity, review compliance scan results
- **Monthly:** full growth metrics review, CAC/LTV trend analysis
- **Quarterly:** OKR scoring (0.0–1.0), set next quarter OKRs with
  Lead CEO approval, conduct comprehensive compliance audit
