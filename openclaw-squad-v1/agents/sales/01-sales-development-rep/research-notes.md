# Research Notes: Best Practices for Elite OpenClaw SDR/BDR Role (March 2026)

## IDENTITY.md
Best practice: Define a clear, human-like persona with domain expertise (e.g., SaaS pipeline builder) and explicit Lead-as-CEO reporting line. Include core values of curiosity and respectful persistence. Citation: Topic 01 (per-agent workspace isolation), Topic 08 (personality core), Topic 21 (Lead oversight), LumaDock persistent-agent pattern (isolated memory prevents bleed). This ensures the agent feels like a top 1% human SDR while maintaining squad hierarchy.

## SOUL.md
Best practice: Four-tier structure (core truths, initiative triggers, self-reflection, Lead protocol) with "no yes-man" clauses and proactive evolution prompts. Keep under 500 tokens for cost control. Citation: Topic 08 (prompt engineering for autonomy), Topic 04 (memory hygiene), Topic 20 (self-improvement loops), Topic 03 (budget enforcement in SOUL). External: LumaDock emphasizes separate memories per persistent agent to avoid tone bleed.

## skills.md
Best practice: Folder-based SKILL.md with YAML frontmatter; limit 3-5 skills per agent; use `disable-model-invocation: true` for high-risk (email send); tiered access with explicit "why" comments; Lead grants via sessions_spawn. Citation: Topic 02 (skill loading/precedence), Topic 19 (4-tier granting), Topic 14 (chaining), Topic 06 (sandbox + deny lists). Why comments prevent cost bloat and security issues per LumaDock tool-policy examples.

## STYLE.md
Best practice: Warm-professional tone with brevity, intelligent pushback on low-fit leads, and personalized openers. Embed examples table for consistency. Citation: Topic 08 (vibe + tone examples), Topic 11 (personality tuning), Topic 05 (loose coordination for natural replies). Aligns with 2026 SDR data showing higher connect rates from empathetic, non-scripted outreach.

## GOALS.md
Best practice: Realistic OKRs tied to 2026 benchmarks (18-21 meetings/month, 2.3-2.5% dial-to-meeting, 53% quota attainment); include evolution targets (weekly script improvement). Citation: Topic 12 (scalability), Topic 03 (budget KPIs), Topic 20 (evolution metrics), external SDR benchmarks (Prospeo.io 2026 data). Lead reviews monthly.

## CONSTRAINTS.md
Best practice: Explicit no-spam rules (max 50 personalized emails/day), GDPR compliance, sandbox "all" mode, cost caps, recursion depth=2. Citation: Topic 06 (security checklist), Topic 17 (legal), Topic 04 (no-recursion), Topic 03 (heartbeat tuning). LumaDock reinforces read-only workspace defaults for sub-agents.

## EVOLUTION.md
Best practice: Weekly reflection -> proposal -> Lead review cycle; never direct SOUL edits; git rollback. Citation: Topic 20 (double-loop learning), Topic 21 (Lead evolution oversight), Topic 15 (backup), Topic 08 (reflection clauses). Production examples show 2-3x quality gains.

## HEARTBEAT.md
Best practice: 4-hour cadence (Lead 1-hour); scan leads for follow-ups; smart silence in groups; proactive but non-spammy triggers. Citation: Topic 03 (heartbeat frequency), Topic 08 (initiative triggers), Topic 05 (opt-in messaging), Topic 09 (context compaction). Prevents token waste while enabling persistence.
