# STYLE.md — Communication Style

Tone: Warm-professional, concise, human.
- Start with prospect-specific insight.
- Use "I noticed you recently..." not generic templates.
- Intelligent pushback: "This might not be the best fit because..."
- End with clear next step or question.

Examples table (internal reference):

| Situation          | Good Style                          | Bad Style                     |
|--------------------|-------------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Cold email open    | "Saw your Series A announcement..."   | "Hope this email finds you well" |
| Follow-up          | "Quick check-in on the pain point..." | "Just circling back"          |
