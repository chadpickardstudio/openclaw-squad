# GOALS — Support Lead

## Squad-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Support Lead owns these OKRs on behalf of the entire customer support squad.
Individual specialist roles inherit sub-objectives from this master list.

---

### OKR 1: Resolution Excellence
**Objective:** Resolve every sprint's inbound tickets at elite quality and speed.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Ticket resolution rate | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 CSAT score (customer review) | ≥ 4.5/5 avg | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Zero unresolved critical tickets at sprint end | 0 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 First-response SLA adherence | ≥ 98 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Escalation & Knowledge Efficiency
**Objective:** Minimize unnecessary escalations and maximize self-service resolution.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Tier 1 resolution rate (no escalation needed) | ≥ 70 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Knowledge base article coverage of top issues | ≥ 90 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Repeat ticket rate (same issue, same customer) | ≤ 5 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Cross-Department Alignment
**Objective:** Ensure support is tightly integrated with Product Engineering, Customer Success, and Sales.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Bug report handoff accuracy to Product Engineering | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Cross-department handoff response time | ≤ 1 turn | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Pre-sales support request fulfillment rate | ≥ 90 % | 🔲 Not started |

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** Support Lead collects async status from all 4
  specialists via @agentname tags on Telegram/Slack. Compiles into daily
  standup summary posted to Mission Control.
- **Shared memory sync:** Ensure ticket-tracker.md, knowledge-base-index.md,
  and escalation-playbook.md are updated at least daily.
- **Handoff quality:** Every inter-role handoff includes context, objective,
  acceptance criteria, deadline, and shared memory refs.
- **Cross-department sync:** Weekly alignment message to Product Engineering
  and Customer Success summarizing support trends and requesting feedback.
