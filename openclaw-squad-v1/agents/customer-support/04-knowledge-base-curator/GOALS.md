# GOALS — Knowledge Base Curator

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Knowledge Base Curator owns these OKRs, which roll up into the Support
Lead's squad-level objectives. Focus: content coverage, quality, and
self-service enablement.

---

### OKR 1: Comprehensive Knowledge Coverage
**Objective:** Ensure the knowledge base covers all recurring support issues with accurate, current content.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 KB article coverage of top 20 issues | ≥ 90 % | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR1.2 New articles published per sprint | ≥ 3 | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR1.3 Article accuracy rate (post-QA review) | ≥ 95 % | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR1.4 Stale article count (> 1 sprint without review) | ≤ 5 % of total | :black_square_button: Not started |

### OKR 2: Ticket Volume Reduction Through Self-Service
**Objective:** Reduce inbound ticket volume by enabling customer and Tier 1 self-service resolution.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Repeat ticket reduction (KB-attributed) | ≥ 10 % sprint-over-sprint | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR2.2 KB article utilization rate (referenced in resolutions) | ≥ 60 % of Tier 1 resolutions | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR2.3 Self-service resolution rate | ≥ 20 % of applicable issues | :black_square_button: Not started |

### OKR 3: Content Quality & Organization
**Objective:** Maintain a well-organized, searchable, consistently formatted knowledge base.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Knowledge-base-index.md completeness | 100 % of articles indexed | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR3.2 Content gap reports addressed within 1 sprint | ≥ 80 % | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR3.3 Article readability score | ≥ target readability level | :black_square_button: Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily status update:** Post KB activity summary to @support-lead via
  @knowledge-base-curator tag for standup compilation.
- **Gap report response:** Acknowledge KB gap reports from @tier1-support-agent
  within 1 interaction turn with priority and estimated publish date.
- **Technical review loop:** Request technical accuracy review from
  @tier2-technical-agent for all technical troubleshooting articles.
- **QA submission:** Submit all new and significantly updated articles to
  @quality-assurance-analyst for content quality review.

## Squad Health Contribution
| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Content gap backlog | ≤ 3 | 4–7 | > 7 |
| Stale articles pending update | ≤ 2 | 3–5 | > 5 |
| Unindexed articles | 0 | 1–2 | 3+ |
| QA review backlog (KB) | ≤ 2 | 3–4 | > 4 |
