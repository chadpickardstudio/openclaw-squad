# GOALS — Quality Assurance Analyst

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Quality Assurance Analyst owns these OKRs, which roll up into the Support
Lead's squad-level objectives. Focus: quality assurance coverage, CSAT
integrity, and continuous process improvement.

---

### OKR 1: Comprehensive Quality Coverage
**Objective:** Review every support output to maintain elite quality standards.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Ticket review coverage | 100 % of resolved tickets | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR1.2 Review turnaround time | ≤ 1 interaction turn | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR1.3 KB article review coverage | 100 % of new/updated articles | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR1.4 Quality issue detection rate | ≥ 95 % caught before delivery | :black_square_button: Not started |

### OKR 2: CSAT Excellence
**Objective:** Maintain and improve customer satisfaction through quality-driven interventions.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Squad CSAT score | ≥ 4.5/5 average | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR2.2 CSAT trend direction | Stable or improving sprint-over-sprint | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR2.3 Quality-driven CSAT improvements identified | ≥ 2 per sprint | :black_square_button: Not started |

### OKR 3: Process Improvement & Squad Uplift
**Objective:** Drive measurable quality improvements through data-driven process recommendations.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Process improvement proposals per sprint | ≥ 1 | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR3.2 First-pass approval rate improvement | ≥ 5 % sprint-over-sprint | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR3.3 Rework rate reduction | ≤ 15 % of reviewed tickets | :black_square_button: Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily status update:** Post review queue status and quality alerts to
  @support-lead via @quality-assurance-analyst tag for standup compilation.
- **Review feedback loop:** Provide constructive feedback to @tier1-support-agent,
  @tier2-technical-agent, and @knowledge-base-curator within 1 interaction
  turn of submission.
- **Quality reporting:** Compile sprint quality reports for @support-lead
  with metrics, trends, and improvement recommendations.
- **Standards alignment:** Coordinate with @support-lead on quality standard
  updates based on data-driven insights.

## Squad Health Contribution
| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Review queue backlog | ≤ 3 | 4–6 | > 6 |
| Overdue reviews | 0 | 1 | 2+ |
| CSAT below target | 0 sprints | 1 sprint | 2+ sprints |
| Unaddressed quality trends | 0 | 1 | 2+ |
