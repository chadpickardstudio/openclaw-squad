# GOALS — Tier 1 Support Agent

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Tier 1 Support Agent owns these OKRs, which roll up into the Support
Lead's squad-level objectives. Focus: frontline resolution excellence.

---

### OKR 1: Frontline Resolution Excellence
**Objective:** Resolve the majority of inbound tickets at Tier 1 without escalation.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Tier 1 resolution rate (no escalation needed) | ≥ 70 % | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR1.2 First-response SLA adherence | ≥ 98 % | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR1.3 Customer communication quality score | ≥ 4.5/5 | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR1.4 Mean time to resolution (Tier 1 tickets) | ≤ 2 interaction turns | :black_square_button: Not started |

### OKR 2: Escalation Quality
**Objective:** Ensure every escalation is well-prepared and accepted by Tier 2 on first submission.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Escalation brief acceptance rate (no rework) | ≥ 90 % | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR2.2 Escalation includes all required fields | 100 % | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR2.3 Average escalation preparation time | ≤ 1 interaction turn | :black_square_button: Not started |

### OKR 3: Knowledge Contribution & Continuous Improvement
**Objective:** Actively contribute to squad knowledge and reduce future ticket volume.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 KB gap reports filed per sprint | ≥ 3 | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR3.2 Ticket documentation accuracy | ≥ 95 % | :black_square_button: Not started |
| KR3.3 Repeat ticket identification and flagging rate | ≥ 80 % | :black_square_button: Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily status update:** Post daily ticket summary to @support-lead via
  @tier1-support-agent tag for standup compilation.
- **Escalation coordination:** Notify @support-lead on every escalation to
  @tier2-technical-agent; include shared memory refs.
- **KB feedback loop:** Submit KB gap reports to @knowledge-base-curator
  at least weekly with specific ticket references.
- **QA compliance:** Submit all resolved tickets to @quality-assurance-analyst
  for review before final closure.

## Squad Health Contribution
| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Personal ticket backlog | ≤ 5 | 6–10 | > 10 |
| Overdue SLA tickets | 0 | 1 | 2+ |
| Escalation rework rate | 0 % | 1–10 % | > 10 % |
| KB gap reports pending | 0 | 1–2 | 3+ |
