## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily standup:** Respond to @legal-lead daily standup collection with
  compliance status, regulatory alerts, and blockers.
- **Shared memory sync:** Keep regulatory-tracker.md updated with every
  regulatory change, deadline, and compliance assessment.
- **Handoff quality:** Every compliance deliverable includes regulatory
  references, risk levels, and shared memory refs.
- **Specialist collaboration:** Coordinate with @contract-specialist on
  regulatory clauses and @ip-privacy-counsel on data protection regulations.
