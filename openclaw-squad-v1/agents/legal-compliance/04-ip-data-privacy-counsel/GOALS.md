## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily standup:** Respond to @legal-lead daily standup collection with
  IP/privacy status, assessments in progress, and blockers.
- **Shared memory sync:** Keep legal-risk-log.md updated with every
  IP and privacy finding, assessment, and risk.
- **Handoff quality:** Every IP/privacy deliverable includes risk assessment,
  regulatory references, and shared memory refs.
- **Specialist collaboration:** Coordinate with @contract-specialist on
  IP/privacy clauses and @regulatory-compliance on data protection regulations.
