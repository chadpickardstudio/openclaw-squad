# GOALS — Contract Specialist

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Contract Specialist owns these OKRs, aligned with the Legal Lead's
squad-level objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Contract Quality & Throughput
**Objective:** Deliver every sprint's contract reviews and drafts at elite quality.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Contract review turnaround | ≤ 2 interaction turns | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Contract accuracy score (Legal Lead review) | ≥ 4.5/5 avg | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Risk clause detection rate | ≥ 95 % of material risks | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 On-time delivery rate | ≥ 90 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Contract Registry & Template Excellence
**Objective:** Maintain a complete, current contract registry and best-in-class templates.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Registry completeness | 100 % of contracts tracked | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Template utilization rate | ≥ 80 % of contracts | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Template improvement proposals | ≥ 1 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Cross-Department Contract Support
**Objective:** Provide responsive, high-quality contract guidance to all departments.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Cross-department response time | ≤ 1 turn | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Department satisfaction score | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Proactive contract risk alerts | ≥ 1 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily standup:** Respond to @legal-lead daily standup collection with
  contract status, blockers, and key findings.
- **Shared memory sync:** Keep contract-registry.md updated with every
  contract action (new, reviewed, amended, expired).
- **Handoff quality:** Every contract deliverable includes risk assessment,
  recommended positions, and shared memory refs.
- **Specialist collaboration:** Coordinate with @regulatory-compliance on
  regulatory clauses and @ip-privacy-counsel on IP/data provisions.
