# GOALS — Strategic Planner

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Strategic Planner owns these OKRs as inherited from the Chief of Staff's
squad-level objectives. These focus on planning quality and analytical depth.

---

### OKR 1: Strategic Planning Excellence
**Objective:** Deliver elite-quality strategic plans that drive organizational success.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Strategic plan delivery rate | 100 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Plan actionability score (CoS rating) | ≥ 4/5 avg | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Scenario coverage for active initiatives | 100 % modeled | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 OKR framework currency | Updated weekly | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Cross-Department Intelligence Integration
**Objective:** Maintain comprehensive data inputs from all departments for informed planning.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Department data freshness | Updated ≥ 3x per week | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Cross-department data sources per sprint | ≥ 3 sources | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Data-to-insight conversion rate | ≥ 80 % of inputs used | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Risk & Opportunity Surfacing
**Objective:** Proactively identify strategic risks and opportunities before they become critical.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Risk assessments completed per initiative | 100 % coverage | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Opportunity briefs surfaced | ≥ 2 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Forecast accuracy (post-sprint review) | ≥ 75 % | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily standup participation:** Respond to @chief-of-staff standup
  collection with current planning tasks, blockers, and highlights.
- **Shared memory sync:** Ensure strategic-plan.md is updated at least
  3x per week with latest planning outputs.
- **Handoff quality:** Every deliverable to @chief-of-staff includes
  context, scenarios, risks, and clear recommendations.
- **Cross-department data sync:** Maintain active data collection
  relationships with all department leads.
