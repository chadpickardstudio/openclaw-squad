# GOALS — Chief of Staff

## Squad-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Chief of Staff owns these OKRs on behalf of the entire executive leadership squad.
Individual specialist roles inherit sub-objectives from this master list.

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** Chief of Staff collects async status from all 3
  specialists via @agentname tags on Telegram/Slack. Compiles into daily
  standup summary posted to Mission Control.
- **Shared memory sync:** Ensure strategic-plan.md, executive-briefing.md, and
  board-calendar.md are updated at least daily.
- **Handoff quality:** Every inter-role handoff includes context, objective,
  acceptance criteria, deadline, and shared memory refs.
- **Cross-department sync:** Weekly alignment message to all department leads
  summarizing executive initiatives and requesting feedback.
