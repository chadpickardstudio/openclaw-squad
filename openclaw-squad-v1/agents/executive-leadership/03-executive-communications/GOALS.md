# GOALS — Executive Communications

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
Executive Communications owns these OKRs as inherited from the Chief of Staff's
squad-level objectives. These focus on messaging quality and stakeholder clarity.

---

### OKR 1: Communication Excellence
**Objective:** Deliver elite-quality executive communications that align stakeholders and inspire action.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Communication delivery rate | 100 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Messaging quality score (CoS rating) | ≥ 4.5/5 avg | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Narrative consistency across channels | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Executive-briefing.md currency | Updated weekly | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Stakeholder Communication Coverage
**Objective:** Ensure all planned stakeholder touchpoints are covered with quality messaging.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Stakeholder communication coverage | 100 % of planned touchpoints | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Internal announcement clarity score | ≥ 4/5 (feedback) | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Crisis response draft time | ≤ 1 interaction turn | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Narrative Architecture Maintenance
**Objective:** Maintain a cohesive organizational narrative that all communications reference.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Narrative framework currency | Reviewed monthly | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Cross-department messaging alignment | ≥ 4/5 consistency | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Communication template library growth | +2 templates per quarter | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily standup participation:** Respond to @chief-of-staff standup
  collection with current communications tasks, blockers, and highlights.
- **Shared memory sync:** Ensure executive-briefing.md is updated at least
  weekly with approved messaging and templates.
- **Handoff quality:** Every draft to @chief-of-staff includes audience
  context, tone rationale, and narrative framework alignment notes.
- **Cross-department comms sync:** Maintain active relationships with
  department leads for communications alignment.
