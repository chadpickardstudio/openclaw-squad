# GOALS — Budget Controller

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Budget Controller owns these OKRs within the finance squad,
aligned to the Finance Lead's master objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Budget Accuracy & Control
**Objective:** Maintain elite budget accuracy and fiscal discipline across all departments.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Budget-to-actual variance | ≤ ±5 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Budget report delivery rate | 100 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Zero unplanned budget overruns | 0 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Departmental budget compliance | 100 % within limits | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Cost Optimization
**Objective:** Proactively identify and realize cost-saving opportunities.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Cost-saving opportunities identified | ≥ 2 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Realized savings vs identified | ≥ 50 % adoption | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Budget request evaluation turnaround | ≤ 1 interaction turn | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Strategic Budget Partnership
**Objective:** Be the trusted budget partner for all departments.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Department satisfaction with budget support | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Budget forecast accuracy | ≥ 90 % within ±10 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Proactive budget advisory notes delivered | ≥ 1 per sprint per dept | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** Respond to @finance-lead daily standup
  collection with budget tracking status, variance alerts, and
  pending budget requests.
- **Shared memory sync:** Update budget-tracker.md with latest budget
  allocations and utilization data at least daily.
- **Handoff quality:** Every budget report to @finance-lead includes
  variance analysis, root causes, and actionable recommendations.
- **Cross-department support:** Respond to departmental budget inquiries
  within 1 interaction turn via @budget-controller tag.
