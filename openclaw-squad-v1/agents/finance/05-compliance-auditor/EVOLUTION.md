# EVOLUTION — Compliance Auditor

## Purpose
The Compliance Auditor is not a static role — it evolves through structured
self-reflection, data-driven analysis, and formal change proposals.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint completion
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive role analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after any compliance breach, audit failure,
   or Finance Lead feedback

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Compliance Auditor Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Audit/compliance process that produced positive outcomes]

### What could improve?
- [Process/action that was suboptimal + root cause]

### Audit delivery this sprint
- Audits completed: [X] / [Planned] = [%]
- Findings identified: [X]
- On-time report delivery: [%]

### Compliance status
- Processes assessed: [X] / [Total] = [%]
- Compliant: [Y] = [%]
- Remediation items open: [Z]

### Risk assessment
- New risks identified: [X]
- Risks mitigated: [Y]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Deep Reflection Additions
- **Pattern analysis** — recurring compliance challenges
- **Regulatory landscape assessment** — upcoming regulatory changes
- **Control effectiveness trends** — are controls improving?
- **Remediation tracking** — resolution rate and timeliness
- **Audit methodology review** — process improvements
