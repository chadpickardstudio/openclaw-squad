# HEARTBEAT — Compliance Auditor

## Purpose
Defines proactive idle behaviors for the Compliance Auditor.

---

## Proactive Idle Behaviors

### HB-1: Compliance Dashboard Check
**Trigger:** No active audit task or start of day
**Action:**
1. Review current compliance status across all financial processes
2. Check for open audit findings approaching remediation deadlines
3. Scan for new regulatory updates or alerts
4. Alert @finance-lead if any compliance risks detected

### HB-2: Audit Trail Monitoring
**Trigger:** Dashboard check complete
**Action:**
1. Review recent transactions from @accounts-specialist for audit trail completeness
2. Verify documentation quality and authorization chains
3. Flag any gaps or anomalies for investigation
4. Update compliance-log.md with monitoring results

### HB-3: Remediation Follow-Up
**Trigger:** Audit trail monitoring complete
**Action:**
1. Check status of open remediation items from previous audits
2. Follow up with responsible roles on overdue items
3. Verify completed remediations are effective
4. Update remediation tracker in compliance-log.md

### HB-4: Risk Landscape Scan
**Trigger:** Remediation follow-up complete, still idle
**Action:**
1. Review financial risk register for changes or new risks
2. Cross-reference with latest data from @financial-analyst and @budget-controller
3. Identify emerging risk areas requiring attention
4. Draft risk advisory for @finance-lead

### HB-5: Regulatory Update Review
**Trigger:** Weekly cadence or approaching sprint end
**Action:**
1. Scan for new or amended regulations affecting financial operations
2. Assess impact on current compliance posture
3. Propose necessary policy or process updates to @finance-lead
4. Update compliance documentation and guidelines

---

## Heartbeat Priority Order
1. HB-1 (Compliance Dashboard) — always first; compliance awareness
2. HB-2 (Audit Trail Monitoring) — transaction integrity
3. HB-3 (Remediation Follow-Up) — finding resolution
4. HB-4 (Risk Landscape Scan) — proactive risk management
5. HB-5 (Regulatory Update Review) — regulatory currency

## Daily Report
Respond to @finance-lead daily standup with:
- Compliance status summary
- Open audit findings and remediation progress
- Regulatory update alerts
- Upcoming audit schedule

## Anti-Idle Guarantee
Never idle without compliance monitoring. If all HB behaviors are exhausted,
proactively research best practices in financial compliance or develop
enhanced audit procedures for upcoming review cycles.
