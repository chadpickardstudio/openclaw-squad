# GOALS — Compliance Auditor

## Role-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Compliance Auditor owns these OKRs within the finance squad,
aligned to the Finance Lead's master objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Audit Excellence
**Objective:** Complete all planned audits with thoroughness and objectivity.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Audit completion rate | 100 % of planned audits | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Audit finding accuracy | ≥ 95 % validated on review | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Audit report delivery | 100 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Issue detection before external audit | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Regulatory Compliance Assurance
**Objective:** Maintain full regulatory compliance across all financial processes.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Regulatory filing timeliness | 100 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Risk assessment coverage | 100 % of financial processes | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Compliance finding resolution time | ≤ 1 sprint | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Compliance Culture Building
**Objective:** Build a proactive compliance culture across the finance squad and organization.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Compliance awareness communications | ≥ 1 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Repeat finding rate | ≤ 5 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Control effectiveness score | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** Respond to @finance-lead daily standup
  collection with audit progress, compliance alerts, and regulatory updates.
- **Shared memory sync:** Update compliance-log.md with latest audit
  findings and compliance status at least daily.
- **Handoff quality:** Every audit report to @finance-lead includes
  evidence-based findings, risk ratings, and actionable remediation plans.
- **Cross-department support:** Respond to compliance inquiries from
  Legal and other departments within 1 interaction turn via
  @compliance-auditor tag.
