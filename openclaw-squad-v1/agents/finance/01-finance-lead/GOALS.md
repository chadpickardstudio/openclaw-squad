# GOALS — Finance Lead

## Squad-Level OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
The Finance Lead owns these OKRs on behalf of the entire finance squad.
Individual specialist roles inherit sub-objectives from this master list.

---

### OKR 1: Financial Reporting Excellence
**Objective:** Deliver every sprint's committed financial reports at elite quality.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Financial report completion rate | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Budget accuracy score (variance within tolerance) | ≥ 95 % within ±5 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Zero unresolved discrepancies escaping to stakeholders | 0 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 On-time report delivery rate | ≥ 90 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Fiscal Discipline & Budget Governance
**Objective:** Maintain best-in-class budget management and fiscal oversight across all departments.
| Key Result | Target | Status |

**Objective:** Ensure full regulatory compliance and proactive risk mitigation.

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily autonomous standup:** Finance Lead collects async status from all 4
  specialists via @agentname tags on Telegram/Slack. Compiles into daily
  standup summary posted to Mission Control.
- **Shared memory sync:** Ensure financial-dashboard.md, budget-tracker.md, and
  compliance-log.md are updated at least daily.
- **Handoff quality:** Every inter-role handoff includes context, objective,
  acceptance criteria, deadline, and shared memory refs.
- **Cross-department sync:** Weekly alignment message to all departments
  summarizing budget status and requesting financial input.
