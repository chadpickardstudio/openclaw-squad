# EVOLUTION — Creative Director

## Purpose
The Creative Director evolves through creative performance analysis,
audience feedback loops, and craft improvement cycles.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive creative analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after brand violations or underperforming creative

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Creative Director Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Creative that drove strong engagement or brand alignment]

### What could improve?
- [Assets that needed excessive revision, missed the mark, etc.]

### Creative quality this sprint
- Assets delivered: [X] / [Assigned] = [%]
- Brand alignment score: [avg]
- Revision rounds: [avg]

### Audience resonance
- Top-performing creative element: [describe]
- Underperforming element: [describe + root cause]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete craft improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Creative trend analysis** — are we keeping up with creative innovation?
- **Brand voice evolution** — does the voice need refreshing?
- **Channel effectiveness** — which channels are our creative strongest on?
- **Collaboration quality** — are Researcher insights translating well?
