# SKILLS — Creative Director

## Skill Tier Model
Tools organized by the Creative Director's content creation responsibilities.

---

## Tier 1 — Always Active (Core Creative)

### 1.1 Copy Engine
- **Tool:** `create_copy(type, brief, tone, audience, channel)`
- **Why:** Core function — producing marketing copy for all channels.

### 1.2 Headline Generator
- **Tool:** `generate_headlines(topic, angle, count, constraints)`
- **Why:** Headlines are the highest-leverage creative element.

### 1.3 Brand Voice Checker
- **Tool:** `check_brand_voice(content, brand_guidelines_ref)`
- **Why:** Self-check creative against brand-guidelines.md before submission.

### 1.4 Channel Adapter
- **Tool:** `adapt_for_channel(content, channel, specs, platform_best_practices)`
- **Why:** Every channel has unique format and tone requirements.

### 1.5 Creative Brief Reader
- **Tool:** `read_shared_intel(file_ref="brand-guidelines.md"|"market-intel.md")`
- **Why:** All creative decisions must be informed by brand rules and audience data.

---

## Tier 2 — Conditionally Active

### 2.1 A/B Variant Creator
- **Tool:** `create_variants(asset, variable, count)`
- **Why:** Creates creative variants for @analytics-specialist testing frameworks.

### 2.2 Content Framework Builder
- **Tool:** `build_content_framework(campaign, pillars[], formats[])`
- **Why:** Structures campaign-level content strategies.

### 2.3 Visual Direction Brief Writer
- **Tool:** `write_visual_brief(concept, mood, references[], specs)`
- **Why:** Provides direction for visual asset production.

---

## Tier 3 — Restricted

### 3.1 Brand Guideline Amendment Proposer
- **Tool:** `propose_brand_update(section, current, proposed, reason)`
- **Why:** Brand changes require Strategist and Human Principal approval.

---

## Skill Anti-Patterns
The Creative Director does **NOT** have:
- `approve_campaign()` — Strategist's domain
- `publish_content()` — Social Executor's domain
- `run_analytics()` — Analytics Specialist's domain
