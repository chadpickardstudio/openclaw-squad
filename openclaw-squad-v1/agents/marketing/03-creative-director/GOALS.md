# GOALS — Creative Director

## Role-Level OKRs

---

### OKR 1: Creative Excellence
**Objective:** Produce best-in-class marketing creative that drives results.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Creative asset delivery rate | 100 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Brand alignment score (Strategist review) | ≥ 4.5/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Revision rounds per asset | ≤ 1 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.4 Creative engagement lift vs baseline | ≥ 10 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Channel Mastery
**Objective:** Create optimized content for every marketing channel.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Channel adaptation coverage | 100 % of required channels | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Platform best practice adherence | ≥ 90 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Channel-specific engagement rate | Above platform median | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Data-Informed Iteration
**Objective:** Continuously improve creative based on performance data.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 A/B test variants delivered per campaign | ≥ 2 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Performance-driven creative iterations | ≥ 1 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Analytics feedback incorporation time | ≤ 1 day | 🔲 Not started |

## Collaboration Goals
- **Per campaign:** Receive brief from @marketing-strategist, develop creative,
  deliver to @social-executor with posting specs.
- **Ongoing:** Incorporate @marketing-researcher audience insights into creative
  development; adapt based on @analytics-specialist performance data.
- **A/B testing:** Provide creative variants to @analytics-specialist for
  testing framework at least 2x per campaign.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @marketing-strategist daily standup
  with current creative pipeline status and blockers.
- **Brand stewardship:** Flag brand-guidelines.md updates needed to
  @marketing-strategist based on creative execution learnings.
