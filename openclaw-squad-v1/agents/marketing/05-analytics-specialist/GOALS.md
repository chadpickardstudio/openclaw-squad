## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily:** Process engagement data from @social-executor; update dashboards;
  report highlights in daily standup to @marketing-strategist.
- **Per campaign:** Deliver performance report to @marketing-strategist;
  provide creative optimization data to @creative-director; provide
  distribution recommendations to @social-executor.
- **Ongoing:** Calibrate measurement frameworks with @marketing-researcher
  benchmark data; maintain A/B testing pipeline.
- **Cross-department:** Share attribution data with Sales for funnel analysis;
  coordinate with Data Analytics department for advanced modeling.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @marketing-strategist daily standup
  with data highlights, anomalies, and pending reports.
