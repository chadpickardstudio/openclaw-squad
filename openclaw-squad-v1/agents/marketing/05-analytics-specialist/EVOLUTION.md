# EVOLUTION — Analytics Specialist

## Purpose
The Analytics Specialist evolves through measurement methodology improvement,
tool optimization, and analytical capability expansion.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive methodology analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after data quality issues or missed anomalies

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Analytics Specialist Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Analyses that drove strong squad decisions]

### What could improve?
- [Missed anomalies, slow reports, methodology gaps]

### Measurement quality this sprint
- Reports delivered: [X] / [Scheduled] = [%]
- A/B tests completed: [X]
- Data accuracy incidents: [X]

### Recommendation effectiveness
- Recommendations adopted: [X] / [Total] = [%]
- Measurable impact from adopted recommendations: [describe]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Methodology review** — are our measurement approaches still valid?
- **Tool effectiveness** — are analytics tools performing optimally?
- **Attribution model accuracy** — calibration check
- **Cross-department data quality** — feedback from Sales and Data Analytics
