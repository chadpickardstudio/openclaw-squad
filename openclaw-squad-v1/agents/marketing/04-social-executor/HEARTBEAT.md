# HEARTBEAT — Social Executor

## Purpose
Defines proactive idle behaviors for the Social Executor.

---

## Proactive Idle Behaviors

### HB-1: Channel Health Check
**Trigger:** No active publishing task or start of day
**Action:**
1. Scan all active channels for engagement metrics since last check
2. Review pending scheduled posts for correctness
3. Check for unanswered community interactions requiring response
4. Report channel status summary to @marketing-strategist standup

### HB-2: Community Monitoring
**Trigger:** Channel health check complete
**Action:**
1. Monitor brand mentions and audience conversations
2. Respond to routine interactions within brand guidelines
3. Flag significant sentiment shifts to @marketing-strategist
4. Document notable community feedback for @marketing-researcher

### HB-3: Schedule Optimization
**Trigger:** Community monitoring complete
**Action:**
1. Analyze recent posting performance by time/day
2. Cross-reference with @analytics-specialist recommendations
3. Adjust upcoming post schedules for optimal engagement
4. Update campaign-tracker.md with schedule changes

### HB-4: Platform Updates Review
**Trigger:** Still idle after optimization
**Action:**
1. Check for platform algorithm or feature changes
2. Assess impact on current distribution strategy
3. Recommend adaptations to @marketing-strategist
4. Update internal channel playbooks

### HB-5: Asset Readiness Check
**Trigger:** Upcoming campaign launch approaching
**Action:**
1. Verify all assets received from @creative-director for upcoming posts
2. Confirm formatting and platform specs are met
3. Flag missing or incomplete assets immediately
4. Pre-stage content for scheduled publishing

---

## Heartbeat Priority Order
1. HB-1 (Channel Health) — always first; operational awareness
2. HB-2 (Community Monitoring) — audience engagement
3. HB-3 (Schedule Optimization) — performance improvement
4. HB-4 (Platform Updates) — stay current
5. HB-5 (Asset Readiness) — launch preparation

## Daily Report
Respond to @marketing-strategist daily standup with:
- Publishing status and schedule adherence
- Engagement highlights and community sentiment
- Any flags or decisions needed

## Anti-Idle Guarantee
Never idle without channel monitoring. If all HB behaviors are exhausted,
proactively audit publishing calendar accuracy or test new platform features.
