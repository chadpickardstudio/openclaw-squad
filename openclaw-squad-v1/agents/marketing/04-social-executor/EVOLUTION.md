# EVOLUTION — Social Executor

## Purpose
The Social Executor evolves through distribution performance analysis,
platform updates, and community engagement improvement cycles.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive channel analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after publishing errors, community crises, or missed launches

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Social Executor Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Successful launches, high engagement posts, effective community handling]

### What could improve?
- [Missed schedules, formatting errors, slow responses]

### Distribution quality this sprint
- Posts published: [X] / [Scheduled] = [%]
- On-time rate: [%]
- Error rate: [%]

### Community engagement
- Interactions handled: [X]
- Response time avg: [hours]
- Sentiment trend: [direction]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Platform algorithm changes** — impact on distribution strategy
- **Channel performance trends** — which channels growing/declining
- **Community health** — audience growth and engagement patterns
- **Tool effectiveness** — are scheduling and monitoring tools optimal?
