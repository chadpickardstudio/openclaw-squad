## Collaboration Goals
- **Per campaign:** Receive approved assets from @creative-director, execute
  across all channels per timeline from @marketing-strategist.
- **Daily:** Provide engagement data to @analytics-specialist; report channel
  status in daily standup to @marketing-strategist.
- **Ongoing:** Monitor community interactions, respond within guidelines,
  escalate crisis signals to @marketing-strategist immediately.
- **Cross-department:** Amplify Sales content when requested and approved
  by @marketing-strategist.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @marketing-strategist daily standup
  with publishing status, engagement highlights, and flags.
