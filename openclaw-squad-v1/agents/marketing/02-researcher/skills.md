# SKILLS — Marketing Researcher

## Skill Tier Model
Tools organized by the Researcher's intelligence-gathering responsibilities.

---

## Tier 1 — Always Active (Core Research)

### 1.1 Market Scanner
- **Tool:** `scan_market(industry, competitors[], signals[])`
- **Why:** Continuous monitoring of competitive landscape and market shifts.

### 1.2 Audience Analyzer
- **Tool:** `analyze_audience(segment, data_sources[], depth)`
- **Why:** Building and maintaining audience personas requires ongoing analysis.

### 1.3 Trend Detector
- **Tool:** `detect_trends(category, timeframe, platforms[])`
- **Why:** Early trend detection gives the squad a competitive edge.

### 1.4 Shared Intel Writer
- **Tool:** `update_shared_intel(file="market-intel.md", section, content, tags[])`
- **Why:** All research findings must flow into shared memory for squad access.

### 1.5 Cross-Department Querier
- **Tool:** `query_department(department, question, context)`
- **Why:** Gathering intel from Sales and Product requires structured queries
  via @agentname tags on Telegram/Slack.

---

## Tier 2 — Conditionally Active

### 2.1 Deep Research Executor
- **Tool:** `deep_research(topic, methodology, scope, deadline)`
- **Why:** Extended research projects beyond routine monitoring.

### 2.2 Benchmark Compiler
- **Tool:** `compile_benchmarks(industry, metrics[], sources[])`
- **Why:** Provides calibration data to @analytics-specialist for measurement.

### 2.3 Report Generator
- **Tool:** `generate_report(type, data, format, audience)`
- **Why:** Structures raw findings into consumable briefs for the squad.

---

## Tier 3 — Restricted

### 3.1 External Data Requester
- **Tool:** `request_external_data(source, justification, cost)`
- **Why:** Paid data sources require Strategist approval.

---

## Skill Anti-Patterns
The Researcher does **NOT** have:
- `approve_campaign()` — Strategist's domain
- `create_creative_asset()` — Creative Director's domain
- `publish_content()` — Social Executor's domain
