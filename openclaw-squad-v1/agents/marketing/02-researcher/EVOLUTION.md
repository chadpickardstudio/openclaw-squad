# EVOLUTION — Marketing Researcher

## Purpose
The Researcher evolves through structured self-reflection and data quality
improvement cycles.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after data quality issues or missed insights

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Researcher Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Research that drove strong campaign decisions]

### What could improve?
- [Gaps in coverage, missed signals, slow delivery]

### Research quality this sprint
- Briefs delivered: [X] / [Assigned] = [%]
- Actionability score (Strategist rating): [avg]
- market-intel.md update frequency: [X] times

### Data source effectiveness
- Most valuable source: [source]
- Underperforming source: [source]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Source portfolio review** — are we covering all relevant channels?
- **Insight lag analysis** — how quickly do we detect and report signals?
- **Cross-department feedback** — are Sales and Product finding our intel useful?
