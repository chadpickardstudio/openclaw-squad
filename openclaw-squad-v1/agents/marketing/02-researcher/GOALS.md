# GOALS — Marketing Researcher

## Role-Level OKRs
The Researcher inherits squad OKRs from the Strategist and owns these
intelligence-specific objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Competitive Intelligence Excellence
**Objective:** Maintain comprehensive, current competitive intelligence.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Competitor activity reports | Weekly delivery | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Competitive positioning matrix freshness | ≤ 7 days old | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Competitive threat alerts delivered | Within 4 hours of detection | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Audience Intelligence
**Objective:** Build and maintain deep audience understanding.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Audience persona updates | Monthly refresh | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Behavioral insight briefs | ≥ 2 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Insight actionability score (Strategist rating) | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Trend Scouting
**Objective:** Surface emerging opportunities before they become mainstream.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Trend reports delivered | ≥ 1 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Trend-to-campaign conversion rate | ≥ 25 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Emerging platform/format assessments | ≥ 1 per month | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily:** Update market-intel.md with any new findings, tagged for relevance.
- **Per campaign:** Deliver research brief to @marketing-strategist within
  assigned deadline, including audience data for @creative-director.
- **Weekly:** Share benchmark data with @analytics-specialist for measurement
  framework calibration.
- **Cross-department:** Gather customer insights from Sales and product
  feedback from Product Engineering at least 2x per sprint.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @marketing-strategist daily standup
  collection with current research status and blockers.
