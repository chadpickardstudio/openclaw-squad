# EVOLUTION — Reporting Specialist

## Purpose
The Reporting Specialist evolves through structured self-reflection and
visualization quality improvement cycles.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after visualization errors or missed delivery deadlines

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — Reporting Specialist Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Dashboard/report that received strong stakeholder feedback]

### What could improve?
- [Delivery delays, visualization issues, stakeholder complaints]

### Delivery quality this sprint
- Reports/dashboards delivered: [X] / [Assigned] = [%]
- Stakeholder satisfaction score: [avg]
- Dashboard uptime: [%]

### Visualization standards
- Style guide compliance: [%]
- Revision requests received: [X]

### Template efficiency
- New reports using templates: [X] / [Total] = [%]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Stakeholder feedback synthesis** — recurring requests and pain points
- **Dashboard performance review** — load times and usage patterns
- **Visual standards audit** — are guidelines current and followed?
