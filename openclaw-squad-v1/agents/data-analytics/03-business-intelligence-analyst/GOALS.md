# GOALS — Business Intelligence Analyst

## Role-Level OKRs
The BI Analyst inherits squad OKRs from the Analytics Lead and owns these
analysis-specific objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Insight Quality & Delivery
**Objective:** Deliver high-impact, accurate business insights on schedule.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Analysis delivery rate | 100 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Insight actionability score (Analytics Lead rating) | ≥ 4/5 | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Query accuracy rate | ≥ 99 % | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Business Metric Coverage
**Objective:** Maintain comprehensive KPI tracking across all departments.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 KPI definitions documented | 100 % of tracked metrics | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Cross-department metric requests fulfilled | ≥ 95 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Metric anomaly detection rate | Alert within 1 cycle of anomaly | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Proactive Intelligence
**Objective:** Surface business opportunities and risks before they are asked for.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Proactive insight briefs delivered | ≥ 2 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Insight-to-action conversion rate | ≥ 30 % | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Exploratory analysis cycles completed | ≥ 1 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily:** Update insights-tracker.md with any new findings, tagged for relevance.
- **Per analysis:** Deliver insight report to @analytics-lead within
  assigned deadline, including validated metrics for @reporting-specialist.
- **Weekly:** Share KPI snapshots with @analytics-lead for cross-department
  distribution.
- **Cross-department:** Fulfill analytical requests from Marketing, Sales,
  and Finance at least 3x per sprint.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @analytics-lead daily standup
  collection with current analysis status and blockers.
