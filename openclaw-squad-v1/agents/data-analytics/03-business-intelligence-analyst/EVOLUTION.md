# EVOLUTION — Business Intelligence Analyst

## Purpose
The BI Analyst evolves through structured self-reflection and analytical
quality improvement cycles.

---

## Self-Reflection Protocol

### Trigger Cadence
1. **Per-Sprint Review** — after every sprint
2. **Monthly Deep Reflection** — comprehensive analysis
3. **Incident-Triggered** — after analytical errors or missed insights

### Per-Sprint Reflection Template
```markdown
## Sprint [N] — BI Analyst Self-Reflection

### What went well?
- [Analysis that drove strong business decisions]

### What could improve?
- [Gaps in coverage, methodology issues, slow delivery]

### Analysis quality this sprint
- Analyses delivered: [X] / [Assigned] = [%]
- Actionability score (Analytics Lead rating): [avg]
- Query accuracy: [%]

### Insight impact
- Insights that led to action: [X] / [Total] = [%]
- Cross-department requests fulfilled: [X]

### Evolution action items
- [ ] [Concrete improvement for next sprint]
```

### Monthly Additions
- **Methodology review** — are analytical approaches keeping up with data complexity?
- **KPI health check** — are tracked metrics still relevant and accurate?
- **Cross-department feedback** — are stakeholders finding analyses useful?
