# GOALS — Data Scientist

## Role-Level OKRs
The Data Scientist inherits squad OKRs from the Analytics Lead and owns these
modeling and experimentation objectives.

---

### OKR 1: Model & Experiment Delivery
**Objective:** Deliver high-quality models and experiments on schedule.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR1.1 Model/experiment delivery rate | 100 % on-time | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.2 Model accuracy meets threshold | 100 % of models | 🔲 Not started |
| KR1.3 Experiment validity rate | 100 % proper statistical design | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 2: Methodological Rigor
**Objective:** Ensure all squad analytics meet statistical best practices.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR2.1 Methodology reviews completed | ≥ 1 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.2 Reproducibility score | 100 % of analyses reproducible | 🔲 Not started |
| KR2.3 Statistical errors caught pre-delivery | 0 errors in released analyses | 🔲 Not started |

### OKR 3: Advanced Analytics Impact
**Objective:** Drive measurable business impact through predictive and experimental insights.
| Key Result | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| KR3.1 Model predictions adopted by business | ≥ 1 per sprint | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.2 Experiment results leading to action | ≥ 50 % of experiments | 🔲 Not started |
| KR3.3 Cross-department advanced analytics requests fulfilled | ≥ 90 % | 🔲 Not started |

---

## Collaboration Goals
- **Daily:** Update insights-tracker.md with experiment progress and model metrics.
- **Per project:** Deliver model/experiment report to @analytics-lead within
  assigned deadline, including outputs for @bi-analyst and @reporting-specialist.
- **Weekly:** Conduct methodology review of squad analytical outputs.
- **Cross-department:** Support Product Engineering A/B testing and Marketing
  attribution modeling as requested.
- **Standup participation:** Respond to @analytics-lead daily standup
  collection with current experiment/model status and blockers.
